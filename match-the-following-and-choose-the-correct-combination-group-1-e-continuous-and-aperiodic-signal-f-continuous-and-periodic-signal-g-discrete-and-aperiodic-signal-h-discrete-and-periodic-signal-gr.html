Guest Question.

Match the following and choose the correct combination.

Group-1
E. Continuous and aperiodic signal
F. Continuous and periodic signal
G. Discrete and aperiodic signal
H. Discrete and periodic signal

Group-2
1. Fourier representation is continuous and periodic
2. Fourier representation is discrete and periodic
3. Fourier representation is continuous and periodic
4. Fourier representation is discrete and periodic

(a) E − 3, F − 2, G − 4, H − 1
(b) E − 1, F − 3, G − 2, H − 4
(c) E − 1, F − 2, G − 3, H − 4
(d) E − 2, F − 1, G − 4, H − 3

Admin
Solution

Continuous and aperiodic signal has continuous and periodic representation.
Continuous and periodic signal has discrete and periodic representation.
Hence, the correct option is (c).
