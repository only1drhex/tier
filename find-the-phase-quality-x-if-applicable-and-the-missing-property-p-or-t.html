Find the phase, quality x if applicable and the missing property P or T.

Solution

a. \(H _{2} O \quad T=120^{\circ} C \quad v=0.5 m ^{3} / kg\)

Table B.1.1 at given T: \(v < v _{ g }=0.89186\)

sat. liq. + vap. \(P = P _{ sat }=198.5 kPa\)

\(x =\left( v – v _{ f }\right) / v _{ fg }\) = (0.5 – 0.00106)/0.8908 = 0.56

\(T =\frac{1.8-1.694}{1.93636-1.694}(150-99.62)+99.62= 1 2 1 . 6 5 ^{\circ} C\)
