Guest Question.

A pair of identical straight-tooth miter gears listed in a catalog has a diametral pitch of 5 at the large end, 25 teeth, a 1.10-in face width, and a 20◦ normal pressure angle; the gears are grade 1 steel through-hardened with a core and case hardness of 180 Brinell. The gears are uncrowned and intended for general industrial use. They have a quality number of \(Q_{v}=7\). It is likely that the application intended will require outboard mounting of the gears. Use a safety factor of 1, a \(10^{7}\) cycle life, and a 0.99 reliability.

(a) For a speed of 600 rev/min find the power rating of this gearset based on AGMA bending strength.

(b) For the same conditions as in part (a) find the power rating of this gearset based on AGMA wear strength.

(c) For a reliability of 0.995, a gear life of \(10^{9}\) revolutions, and a safety factor of \(S_{F}=S_{H}=1.5\), find the power rating for this gearset using AGMA strengths.

Admin
Solution

From Figs. 15–14 and 15–15,

 

\(d_{P}=N_{P} / P=25 / 5=5.000 \text { in }\)

 

\(v_{t}=\pi d_{P} n_{P} / 12=\pi(5) 600 / 12=785.4 ft / min\)

 

Overload factor: uniform-uniform loading, Table 15–2, \(K_{o}=1.00\).

 

Safety factor: \(S_{F}=1, S_{H}=1\).

 

Dynamic factor \(K_{v}\): from Eq. (15–6),

 

Table 15–2 Overload Factors \(K_{o}\left(K_{A}\right)\) Source: ANSI/AGMA 2003-B97.
Character of Prime Mover Character of Load on Driven Machine
Uniform Light Shock Medium Shock Heavy Shock
Heavy Shock 1.00 1.25 1.5 1.75 or highe
Light shock 1.10 1.35 1.60 1.85 or higher
Medium shock 1.25 1.50 1.75 2.00 or higher
Heavy shock 1.50 1.75 2.00 2.25 or higher

 

Note: This table is for speed-decreasing drives. For speed-increasing drives, add 0.01\(( N / n )^{2} \text { or } 0.01\left( z _{2} / z _{1}\right)^{2}\) to the above factors.

 

\(\begin{array}{l}A=50+56(1-B) \\B=0.25\left(12-Q_{v}\right)^{2 / 3}\end{array}\) (15–6)

 

\(B=0.25(12-7)^{2 / 3}=0.731\)

 

\(A=50+56(1-0.731)=65.06\)

 

\(K_{v}=\left(\frac{65.06+\sqrt{785.4}}{65.06}\right)^{0.731}=1.299\)

 

From Eq. (15–8),

 

\(\begin{aligned}v_{t \max } &=\left[A+\left(Q_{v}-3\right)\right]^{2} \quad(\text { U.S. customary units }) \\v_{\text {et } \max } &=\frac{\left[A+\left(Q_{v}-3\right)\right]^{2}}{\text { ano }} \quad(\text { SI units })\end{aligned}\) (15–8)

 

\(v_{t \max }=[65.06+(7-3)]^{2}=4769 ft / \min\)

 

\(v_{t}<v_{t \max }\), that is, 785.4 < 4769 ft/min, therefore \(K_{v}\) is valid. From Eq. (15–10),

 

\(\begin{aligned}K_{s} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}0.4867+0.2132 / P_{d} & 0.5 \leq P_{d} \leq 16 \text { teeth/in } \\0.5 & P_{d}>16 \text { teeth/in }\end{array}\right. \text { (U.S. customary units) }\\Y_{x} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}0.5 & m_{e t}<1.6 mm \\0.4867+0.008339 m_{e t} & 1.6 \leq m_{e t} \leq 50 mm\end{array}\right. \text { (SI units) }\end{aligned}\) (15–10)

 

\(K_{s}=0.4867+0.2132 / 5=0.529\)

 

From Eq. (15–11),

 

\(\begin{array}{ll}K_{m}=K_{m b}+0.0036 F^{2} & \text { (U.S. customary units) } \\K_{H \beta}=K_{m b}+5.6\left(10^{-6}\right) b^{2} & \text { (SI units) }\end{array}\) (15–11)

 

\(K_{m b}=1.25 \quad \text { and } \quad K_{m}=1.25+0.0036(1.10)^{2}=1.254\)

 

From Eq. (15–13), \(K_{x}=1\). From Fig. 15–6, I = 0.065; from Fig. 15–7, \(J_{P}=0.216\), \(J_{G}=0.216\). From Eq. (15–15),

 

\(K_{x}=Y_{\beta}=1\) (15–13)

 

\(\begin{aligned}K_{L} &=\left\{\begin{array}{lll}2.7 & 10^{2} \leq N_{L}<10^{3} \\6.1514 N_{L}^{-0.1182} & 10^{3} \leq N_{L}<3\left(10^{6}\right) & \\1.6831 N_{L}^{-0.0323} & 3\left(10^{6}\right) \leq N_{L} \leq 10^{10} & \text { general } \\1.3558 N_{L}^{-0.0178} & 3\left(10^{6}\right) \leq N_{L} \leq 10^{10} & \text { critical }\end{array}\right.\\Y_{N T} &=\left\{\begin{array}{lll}2.7 & 10^{2} \leq n_{L}<10^{3} & \\6.1514 n_{L}^{-0.1182} & 10^{3} \leq n_{L}<3\left(10^{6}\right) & \\1.6831 n_{L}^{-0.0323} & 3\left(10^{6}\right) \leq n_{L} \leq 10^{10} & \text { general } \\1.3558 n_{L}^{-0.0323} & 3\left(10^{6}\right) \leq n_{L} \leq 10^{10} & \text { critical }\end{array}\right.\end{aligned}\) (15–15)

 

\(K_{L}=1.683\left(10^{7}\right)^{-0.0323}=0.99996 \doteq 1\)

 

From Eq. (15–14),

 

\(\begin{aligned}C_{L} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}2 & 10^{3} \leq N_{L}<10^{4} \\3.4822 N_{L}^{-0.0602} & 10^{4} \leq N_{L} \leq 10^{10}\end{array}\right.\\Z_{N T} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}2 & 10^{3} \leq n_{L}<10^{4} \\3.4822 n_{L}^{-0.0602} & 10^{4} \leq n_{L} \leq 10^{10}\end{array}\right.\end{aligned}\) (15–14)

 

\(C_{L}=3.4822\left(10^{7}\right)^{-0.0602}=1.32\)

 

Since \(H_{B P} / H_{B G}=1\), then from Fig. 15–10, \(C_{H}=1\). From Eqs. (15–13) and (15–18), \(K_{x}=1 \text { and } K_{T}=1\) respectively. From Eq. (15–20)

 

\(\begin{aligned}K_{T} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}1 & 32^{\circ} F \leq t \leq 250^{\circ} F \\(460+t) / 710 & t>250^{\circ} F\end{array}\right.\\K_{\theta} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}1 & 0^{\circ} C \leq \theta \leq 120^{\circ} C \\(273+\theta) / 393 & \theta>120^{\circ} C\end{array}\right.\end{aligned}\) (15–18)

 

\(Y_{Z}=K_{R}=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}0.50-0.25 \log (1-R) & 0.99 \leq R \leq 0.999 \\0.70-0.15 \log (1-R) & 0.90 \leq R<0.99\end{array}\right.\) (15–20)

 

\(K_{R}=0.70-0.15 \log (1-0.99)=1, \quad C_{R}=\sqrt{K}_{R}=\sqrt{1}=1\)

 

(a) Bending: From Eq. (15–23),

 

\(\begin{array}{ll}s_{a t}=44 H_{B}+2100 psi & \text { grade } 1 \\\sigma_{F \lim }=0.30 H_{B}+14.48 MPa & \text { grade } 1 \\s_{a t}=48 H_{B}+5980 psi & \text { grade } 2 \\\sigma_{H \lim }=0.33 H_{B}+41.24 MPa & \text { grade } 2\end{array}\) (15–23)

 

\(s_{a t}=44(180)+2100=10020 \text { psi }\)

 

From Eq. (15–3),

 

\(s_{t}=\frac{W^{t}}{F} P_{d} K_{o} K_{v} \frac{K_{s} K_{m}}{K_{x} J}\) (U.S. customary units) (15–3)

 

\(\begin{aligned}s_{t} &=\sigma=\frac{W^{t}}{F} P_{d} K_{o} K_{v} \frac{K_{s} K_{m}}{K_{x} J}=\frac{W^{t}}{1.10}(5)(1) 1.299 \frac{0.529(1.254)}{(1) 0.216} \\&=18.13 W^{t}\end{aligned}\)

 

From Eq. (15–4),

 

\(s_{w t}=\frac{s_{a t} K_{L}}{S_{F} K_{T} K_{R}}\) (U.S. customary units) (15–4)

 

\(s_{w t}=\frac{s_{a t} K_{L}}{S_{F} K_{T} K_{R}}=\frac{10020(1)}{(1)(1)(1)}=10020 psi\)

 

Equating \(s_{t} \text { and } s_{w t}\)

 

\(18.13 W^{t}=10020 \quad W^{t}=552.6 lbf\)

 

\(H=\frac{W^{t} v_{t}}{33000}=\frac{552.6(785.4)}{33000}=13.2 hp\)

 

(b) Wear: From Fig. 15–12,

 

\(s_{a c}=341(180)+23620=85000 psi\)

 

From Eq. (15–2),

 

\(s_{w c}=\left(\sigma_{c}\right)_{ all }=\frac{s_{a c} C_{L} C_{H}}{S_{H} K_{T} C_{R}}\) (U.S. customary units) (15–2)

 

\(\sigma_{c, \text { all }}=\frac{s_{a c} C_{L} C_{H}}{S_{H} K_{T} C_{R}}=\frac{85000(1.32)(1)}{(1)(1)(1)}=112200 psi\)

 

Now \(C_{p}=2290 \sqrt{ psi }\) from definitions following Eq. (15–21). From Eq. (15–9)

 

\(\begin{aligned}C_{s} &=\left\{\begin{array}{lll}0.5 & F<0.5 in \\0.125 F+0.4375 & 0.5 \leq F \leq 4.5 in & \text { (U.S. customary units) } \\1 & F>4.5 in\end{array}\right.\\Z_{x}=\left\{\begin{array}{lll}0.5 & b<12.7 mm \\0.00492 b+0.4375 & 12.7 \leq b \leq 114.3 mm & \text { (SI units) } \\1 & b>114.3 mm\end{array}\right.\end{aligned}\) (15–9)

 

\(\begin{array}{l}C_{p}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{\pi\left[\left(1-v_{P}^{2}\right) / E_{P}+\left(1-v_{C}^{2}\right) / E_{C}\right]}} \\Z_{E}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{\pi\left[\left(1-v_{1}^{2}\right) / E_{1}+\left(1-v_{2}^{2}\right) / E_{2}\right]}}\end{array}\) (15–21)

 

\(C_{s}=0.125(1.1)+0.4375=0.575\)

 

From Eq. (15–12), \(C_{x c}=2\). Substituting in Eq. (15–1) gives

 

\(C_{x c}=Z_{x c}=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}1.5 & \text { properly crowned teeth } \\2.0 & \text { or larger uncrowned teeth }\end{array}\right.\) (15–12)

 

\(s_{c}=\sigma_{c}=C_{p}\left(\frac{W^{t}}{F d_{P} I} K_{o} K_{v} K_{m} C_{s} C_{x c}\right)^{1 / 2}\) (U.S. customary units) (15–1)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\sigma_{c} &=C_{p}\left(\frac{W^{t}}{F d_{P} I} K_{o} K_{v} K_{m} C_{s} C_{x c}\right)^{1 / 2} \\&=2290\left[\frac{W^{t}}{1.10(5) 0.065}(1) 1.299(1.254) 0.575(2)\right]^{1 / 2}=5242 \sqrt{W^{t}}\end{aligned}\)

 

Equating \(\sigma_{c} \text { and } \sigma_{c}\),all gives

 

\(5242 \sqrt{W^{t}}=112200, \quad W^{t}=458.1 lbf\)

 

\(H=\frac{458.1(785.4)}{33000}=10.9 hp\)

 

Rated power for the gearset is

 

\(H=\min (12.9,10.9)=10.9 hp\)

 

(c) Life goal \(10^{9}\) cycles, R = 0.995, \(S_{F}=S_{H}=1.5\), and from Eq. (15 15),

 

\(K_{L}=1.683\left(10^{9}\right)^{-0.0323}=0.8618\)

 

From Eq. (15–19),

 

\(Y_{Z}=K_{R}=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}0.50-0.25 \log (1-R) & 0.99 \leq R \leq 0.999 \\0.70-0.15 \log (1-R) & 0.90 \leq R<0.99\end{array}\right.\) (15–19)

 

\(K_{R}=0.50-0.25 \log (1-0.995)=1.075, \quad C_{R}=\sqrt{K_{R}}=\sqrt{1.075}=1.037\)

 

From Eq. (15–14),

 

\(\begin{aligned}C_{L} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}2 & 10^{3} \leq N_{L}<10^{4} \\3.4822 N_{L}^{-0.0602} & 10^{4} \leq N_{L} \leq 10^{10}\end{array}\right.\\Z_{N T} &=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}2 & 10^{3} \leq n_{L}<10^{4} \\3.4822 n_{L}^{-0.0602} & 10^{4} \leq n_{L} \leq 10^{10}\end{array}\right.\end{aligned}\) (15–14)

 

\(C_{L}=3.4822\left(10^{9}\right)^{-0.0602}=1\)

 

Bending: From Eq. (15–23) and part (a), sat = 10 020 psi. From Eq. (15–3),

 

\(s_{t}=\sigma=\frac{W^{t}}{1.10} 5(1) 1.299 \frac{0.529(1.254)}{(1) 0.216}=18.13 W^{t}\)

 

From Eq. (15–4),

 

\(s_{w t}=\frac{s_{a t} K_{L}}{S_{F} K_{T} K_{R}}=\frac{10020(0.8618)}{1.5(1) 1.075}=5355 psi\)

 

Equating \(S_{t} \operatorname{to} S_{w t}\) gives

 

\(18.13 W^{t}=5355 \quad W^{t}=295.4 lbf\)

 

\(H=\frac{295.4(785.4)}{33000}=7.0 hp\)

 

Wear: From Eq. (15–22), and part (b), \(s_{a c}=85000\) psi.

 

\(\begin{array}{ll}s_{a c}=341 H_{B}+23620 psi & \text { grade } 1 \\\sigma_{H \lim }=2.35 H_{B}+162.89 MPa & \text { grade } 1 \\s_{a c}=363.6 H_{B}+29560 psi & \text { grade } 2 \\\sigma_{H \lim }=2.51 H_{B}+203.86 MPa & \text { grade } 2\end{array}\) (15–22)

 

Substituting into Eq. (15–2) gives

 

\(\sigma_{c, \text { all }}=\frac{s_{a c} C_{L} C_{H}}{S_{H} K_{T} C_{R}}=\frac{85000(1)(1)}{1.5(1) 1.037}=54640 psi\)

 

Substituting into Eq. (15–1) gives, from part (b), \(\sigma_{c}=5242 \sqrt{W^{t}}\) .

Equating \(\sigma_{c} \operatorname{to} \sigma_{c}\),all gives

 

\(\sigma_{c}=\sigma_{c, \text { all }}=54640=5242 \sqrt{W^{t}} \quad W^{t}=108.6 lbf\)

 

The wear power is

 

\(H=\frac{108.6(785.4)}{33000}=2.58 hp\)

 

The mesh rated power is H = min(7.0, 2.58) = 2.6 hp.
