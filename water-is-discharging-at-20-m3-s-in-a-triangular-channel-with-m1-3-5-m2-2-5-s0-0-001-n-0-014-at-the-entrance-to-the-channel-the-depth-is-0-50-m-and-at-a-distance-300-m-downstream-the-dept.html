Water is discharging at 20 \(m ^{3} / s\) in a triangular channel with \(m_{1}=3.5, m_{2}=2.5, S_{0}=0.001\), n = 0.014. At the entrance to the channel the depth is 0.50 m, and at a distance 300 m downstream the depth is 2.5 m. Determine the nature of the water surface over the 300-m reach.
