Guest Question.

A cantilever beam projects 1.5 m from its support and carries a uniformly distributed load of 16 kN/m over its whole length together with a load of 30 kN at 0.75 m from the support. The outer end rests on a prop which compresses 0.12 mmfor every kN of compressive load. If the value of EI for the beam is \(2000 kNm ^{2}\), determine the reaction in the prop.
Ans. 23.4 kN.

Admin
Solution

Using the moment-area method and referring to Fig. S.13.29, Eq. (13.10),

\(x_{ B }\left(\frac{ d v}{ d x}\right)_{ B }-x_{ A }\left(\frac{ d v}{ d x}\right)_{ A }-\left(v_{ B }-v_{ A }\right)=\int_{ A }^{ B } \frac{M}{E I} x d x\) (13.10)

with the origin for x at B, becomes

\(v_{ B }=0.12 \times 10^{-3} R_{ B }=\left(\frac{1}{E I}\right)\left[\int_{0}^{0.75}\left(R_{ B } x-\frac{16 x^{2}}{2}\right) x d x+\int_{0.75}^{1.5}\left(R_{ B } x-\frac{16 x^{2}}{2}-30(x-0.75)\right) x d x\right]\)

i.e.

\(E I \times 0.12 \times 10^{-3} R_{ B }=\left[\frac{R_{ B } x^{3}}{3}-2 x^{4}\right]_{0}^{0.75}-30\left[\frac{x^{3}}{3}-\frac{0.75 x^{2}}{2}\right]_{0.75}^{1.5}\)

which gives

\(R_{ B }=23.4 kN\).
