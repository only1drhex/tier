Guest Question.

A 150 kVA transformer 2400/240 V rating has the following parameters:
\(R_{1} = 0.2\Omega\) , \(R_{2} = 2\times 10^{-3}\)
\(X_{1} = 0.45\Omega\) , \(X_{2} = 4.5 \times10^{-3}\)
\(R_{i} = 10 k\Omega\) ,\(X_{m} = 1.6 k\Omega\) (referred to HV)
Calculate the leakage inductances, magnetizing inductance, mutual inductance and self-inductances.

Admin
Solution

\(a=\frac{N_{1} }{N_{2} } \approx \frac{2400}{240} =10\)
\(X_{1} = 2 \pi fl_{1} ,l_{1}=\frac{0.45}{314} \times 10^{-3} = 0.01433\) mH
\(X_{2}= 2 \pi fl_{2} ,l_{2}=\frac{4.5\times 10^{-3} }{314} = 0.01433\) mH
Magnetizing inductance
\(2 \pi fL_{m1}=X_{m} =1.6\times 10^{3} \)
\(L_{m1}=5.096\) H
Self inductances
\(l_{1} =L_{1} -L_{m1}\)
\(L_{1}=5.096 + 0.01433 \times 10^{–3} = 5.096\) H
\(L_{m1}=aM,M=\frac{L_{m1}}{a} =\frac{5.096}{10}=0.5096\) H
\(l_{2}=L_{2}-\frac{M}{a} \)
\(L_{2}=l_{2}+ \frac{M}{a} = 0.01433\times 10^{-3} + \frac{0 5096}{10} =0.05098\) H
Coupling factor \(k=\frac{M}{\sqrt{L_{1} L_{2}} }= \frac{0.5096}{\sqrt{5.096\times 0.05098} } =0.09998\simeq 1\)
