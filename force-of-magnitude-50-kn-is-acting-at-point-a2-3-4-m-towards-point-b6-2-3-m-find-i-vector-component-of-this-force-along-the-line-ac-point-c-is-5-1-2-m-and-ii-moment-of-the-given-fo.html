Guest Question.

Force of magnitude 50 kN is acting at point A(2, 3, 4) m towards point B(6, -2, -3) m. Find (i) Vector component of this force along the line AC. Point C is (5, 1, 2) m and (ii) Moment of the given force about a point D(-1, 1, 2) m.

Admin
Solution

(i) Vector component of force along the line AC \( \overline{F}_{AC} \)

(a) Force vector \( \overline{F} \)

\( \overline{F} = (F) ( \overline{e}_{AB}) \).

\( \overline{F} = (50) \biggl(\frac{4 i -5 j -7k}{\sqrt{4^2+(-5)^2+(-7)^2} }\biggr) \).

\( \overline{F} = 21.08 i – 26.35 j – 36.89 k \).

(b) Unit vector \( ( \overline{e}_{AC}) \)

\( ( \overline{e}_{AC}) = \frac{\overline{AC} }{\left|\overline{AC} \right| } =\frac{3 i – 2 j – 2 k}{\sqrt{3^2+2^2+2^2}} \) .

\( ( \overline{e}_{AC}) = 0.727 i – 0.485 j – 0.485 k \).

(c) Scalar component (\( F_{AC} \))

(Magnitude of force along given line AC )

\( F_{AC} =\overline{F} . \overline{e}_{AC} \) (Dot product)

\( F_{AC} = (21.08 i – 26.35 j – 36.89 k ) . (0.727 i – 0.485 j – 0.485 k ) \).

\( F_{AC} = 44.95 \) kN.

(d) Vector component of force along the line AC (\( F_{AC} \) ).

\( \overline{F}_{AC} = (F_{AC}) (\overline{e}_{AC}) \).

\( \overline{F}_{AC} = (44.95)(0.727 i – 0.485 j – 0.485 k) \).

\( \overline{F}_{AC} = 33.44 i – 22.31 j – 22.31 \) k (kN) Ans.

(ii) Moment of F about a point D (\( \overline{M}_{D}\))

(a) Position vector (\( \overline{r}_{DA}\))

\( \overline{r}_{DA} = \overline{DA} \).

\( \overline{r}_{DA} = 3 i + 2 j + 2 k \).

(b) Force vector \( \overline{F} \)

\( \overline{F} = 21.08 i – 26.35 j – 36.89 k \).

(c) Moment of \( \overline{F}\) about a point D (\( \overline{M}_{D}\))

\( \overline{M}_{D} = \overline{r}_{DA} Ã—\overline{F} \) (cross product)

\( \overline{M}_{D} = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k\\ 3 &2 &2 \\ 21.28 & -26.35 & -36.89 \end{vmatrix} \)

\( \overline{M}_{D} = -21.08 i + 152.83 j – 121.21 \) k (kN-m). Ans.
