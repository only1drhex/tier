Guest Question.

A constant strain triangular element has corners 1(0, 0), 2(4, 0) and 3(2, 2) referred to a Cartesian Oxy axes system and is 1 unit thick. If the elasticity matrix [D] has elements \(D_{11}=D_{22}=a, D_{12}=D_{21}=b\), \(D_{13}=D_{23}=D_{31}=D_{32}=0\) and \(D_{33}=c\), derive the stiffness matrix for the element.

Admin
Solution

From Eq. (17.69)

\(\left.\begin{array}{l}w(x, y)=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2} x+\alpha_{3} y \\v(x, y)=\alpha_{4}+\alpha_{5} x+\alpha_{6} y\end{array}\right\}\) (17.69)

\(w_{1}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(0)+\alpha_{3}(0)\)

i.e.

\(w_{1}=\alpha_{1}\)

 

\(w_{2}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(4)+\alpha_{3}(0)\) (i)

i.e.

\(w_{2}=\alpha_{1}+4 \alpha_{2}\)

 

\(w_{3}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(2)+\alpha_{3}(2)\) (ii)

i.e.

\(w_{3}=\alpha_{1}+2 \alpha_{2}+2 \alpha_{3}\) (iii)

From Eq. (i)

\(\alpha_{1}=w_{1}\) (iv)

and from Eqs (ii) and (iv)

\(\alpha_{2}=\frac{w_{2}-w_{1}}{4}\) (v)

Then, from Eqs (iii)â€“(v)

\(\alpha_{3}=\frac{2 w_{3}-w_{1}-w_{2}}{4}\) (vi)

Substituting for \(\alpha_{1}, \alpha_{2}\) and \(\alpha_{3}\) in the first of Eq. (17.69)

in the first of Eq. (17.69) gives

\(w=w_{1}+\left(\frac{w_{2}-w_{1}}{4}\right) x+\left(\frac{2 w_{3}-w_{1}-w_{2}}{4}\right) y\)

or

\(w=\left(1-\frac{x}{4}-\frac{y}{4}\right) w_{1}+\left(\frac{x}{4}-\frac{y}{4}\right) w_{2}+\frac{y}{2} w_{3}\) (vii)

Similarly

\(v=\left(1-\frac{x}{4}-\frac{y}{4}\right) v_{1}+\left(\frac{x}{4}-\frac{y}{4}\right) v_{2}+\frac{y}{2} v_{3}\) (viii)

Now from Eq. (17.75)

\(\varepsilon_{x}=\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} \quad \varepsilon_{y}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y} \quad \gamma_{x y}=\frac{\partial w}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\) (17.75)

\(\varepsilon_{x}=\frac{\partial w}{\partial x}=-\frac{w_{1}}{4}+\frac{w_{2}}{4}\)

 

\(\varepsilon_{y}=\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}=-\frac{v_{1}}{4}-\frac{v_{2}}{4}+\frac{v_{3}}{2}\)

and

\(\gamma_{x y}=\frac{\partial w}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=-\frac{w_{1}}{4}-\frac{w_{2}}{4}-\frac{v_{1}}{4}+\frac{v_{2}}{4}\)

Hence

\([B]\left\{\delta^{ e }\right\}=\left[\begin{array}{c}\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} \\\frac{\partial v}{\partial y} \\\frac{\partial w}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\end{array}\right]=\frac{1}{4}\left[\begin{array}{rrrrrr}-1 & 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\0 & -1 & 0 & -1 & 0 & 2 \\-1 & -1 & -1 & 1 & 2 & 0\end{array}\right]\left\{\begin{array}{c}w_{1} \\v_{1} \\w_{2} \\v_{2} \\w_{3} \\v_{3}\end{array}\right\}\) (ix)

Also

\([D]=\left[\begin{array}{lll}a & b & 0 \\b & a & 0 \\0 & 0 & c\end{array}\right]\)

Hence

\([D][B]=\frac{1}{4}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}-a & -b & a & -b & 0 & 2 b \\-b & -a & b & -a & 0 & 2 a \\-c & -c & -c & c & 2 c & 0\end{array}\right]\)

and

\([B]^{ T }[D][B]=\frac{1}{16}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}a+b & b+c & -a+c & b-c & -2 c & -2 b \\b+c & a+c & -b+c & a-c & -2 c & -2 a \\-a+c & -b+c & a+c & -b-c & -2 c & 2 b \\b-c & a-c & -b-c & a+c & 2 c & -2 a \\-2 c & -2 c & -2 c & 2 c & 4 c & 0 \\-2 b & -2 a & 2 b & -2 a & 0 & 4 a\end{array}\right]\)

Then, from Eq. (17.81)

\(\left[K^{ e }\right]=\left[[B]^{ T }[D][B] A t\right]\) (17.81)

\(\left[K^{ e }\right]=\frac{1}{4}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}a+c & b+c & -a+c & b-c & -2 c & -2 b \\b+c & a+c & -b+c & a-c & -2 c & -2 a \\-a+c & -b+c & a+c & -b-c & -2 c & 2 b \\b-c & a-c & -b-c & a+c & 2 c & -2 a \\-2 c & -2 c & -2 c & 2 c & 4 c & 0 \\-2 b & -2 a & 2 b & -2 a & 0 & 4 a\end{array}\right]\)
