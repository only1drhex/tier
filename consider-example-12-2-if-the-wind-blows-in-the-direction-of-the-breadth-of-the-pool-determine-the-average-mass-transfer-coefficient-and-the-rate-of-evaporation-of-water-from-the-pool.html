Guest Question.

Consider . If the wind blows in the direction of the breadth of the pool, determine the average mass transfer coefficient and the rate of evaporation of water from the pool.

Admin
Solution
\(Re_{L}=\frac{u_{\infty }L}{v}=\frac{0.7\times 4}{0.63 \times 10^{-5}}=1.71\times 10^{5}\)

Since \(Re_{L}\) < 5Ã—\(10^{5}\) , the flow is laminar.

\(\bar{Sh}_{L}=0.664Re_{L}^{1/2}Sc_{1/3}=0.664 \times (1.71 \times 10^{5})^{1/2}\times 0.619^{1/3}=234\)

 

\(\bar{h}_{mL}= \bar{Sh}_{L}\frac{D_{AB}}{L} =234\times \frac{2.63 \times 10^{-5}}{4}=1.538 \times 10^{-3}m/s \)

The rate of evaporation is

\(\dot{m}_{vapor}=\bar{h}_{mL}A(\rho _{vapor,s}-\rho _{vapor,\infty }) =1.538 \times10^{-3}\times24\times4\times(0.03-0.0121)=2.64\times10^{-3} kg/s=228.3 kg/day\)
