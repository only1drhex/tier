Solution

From Table 11–2, for a 02-70 mm deep-groove ball bearing, \(C_{10}\) = 61.8 kN = 13 888 lbf. Using Eq. (11–19), recalling from that \(a_{f} = 1.2, F_{D} = 413 lbf, x_{0} = 0.02, (θ − x_{0}) = 4.439\), and b = 1.489, we can write

\(R \dot{=} 1 − \left\{ \frac {x_{D} \left( \frac {a_{f} F_{D}}{C_{10}}\right)^{a}− x_{0}}{θ − x_{0}}\right\}^{b} R ≥ 0.90 \) (11–19)

\( R \dot{=}1 − \left\{ \frac {\left[ 540 \left[\frac{1.2(413)}{13 888 } \right]^{3} − 0.02 \right] }{4.439}\right\}^{1.489} = 0.999 965\)

which, as expected, is much higher than 0.99 from .
