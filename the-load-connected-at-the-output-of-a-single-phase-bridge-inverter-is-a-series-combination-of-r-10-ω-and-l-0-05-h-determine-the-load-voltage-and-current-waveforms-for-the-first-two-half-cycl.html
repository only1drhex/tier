Guest Question.

The load connected at the output of a single-phase bridge inverter is a series combination of \( R=10 \Omega\) and L = 0.05 H. Determine the load voltage and current waveforms for the first two half-cycles with rectangular wave output at 50 Hz. The input to the inverter is \( V_{d c}=250 V \).

Also find the expression for steady-state current in each half-cycle.

Admin
Solution

Load time-constant, \( \tau=L / R=\frac{0.05}{10}=0.005 s\)

Half-cycle time \( =\frac{1}{100} s\)

For the first half-cycle, initial current is zero

\( v_{0}=+250 V\)

 

\( i_{0}=25-25 e^{-t / \tau}\)

 

\( =25\left(1-e^{-200 x}\right)\)

 

\( i_{0}\) (at end of first half-cycle) \( =25\left(1-e^{-2}\right)\)

 

For the second half-cycle.

\( v_{0}=-250 V\)

 

\( i_{0}=-25+\left[25\left(1-e^{-2}\right)\right] e^{-200 t}\)

 

Steady-state current With reference to Fig. 12.47(e), during the positive half-cycle

\( i_{0}=25-\left(25+I_{01}\right) e^{-200 t}\)

 

\( i _{0}(T / 2)=25-\left(25+I_{01}\right) e^{-2}=1_{01}\)

 

Or \( I_{01}=25 \times \frac{1-e^{-2}}{1+e^{-2}}=19\)

 

Hence \( i_{0}=25-(25+19) e^{-200 t}\)

 

\( =25-44 e^{-200 t}\) (+ve half-cycle)

\( =-25+44 e^{-200 t}\) (–ve half-cycle)
