Guest Question.

A 50 kW, 250 V, 200 A long shunt cumulative compound dc generator has the following data:

\( R_{a}\) (inclusive of brush voltage drop) = 0.05 \( \Omega\)

Series field resistance \( R_{se} = 0.01 \Omega\)

The magnetization characteristic at 1200 rpm is drawn in Fig. 7.62(a)

Shunt field turns, \( N_{f} = 1000\)

Series field turns, \( N_{se} = 3\)

The generator is run at 1150 rpm and its shunt field current is adjusted to 5.6 A.

Compute the terminal voltage at rated voltage current

Admin
Solution

The connection diagram of long shunt compound generator is drawn in Fig. 7.62(b)

Load current, \( I_{L} = 200 A\)

 

\( I_{a} = 200 + 5.6 = 205.6 A\)

 

Excitation ampere-turns \( 1000 \times 5.6 + 205.6 \times 3 = 6217\)

 

Equivalent shunt field current, \( I_{f,eq} = \frac {6217} {1000} \approx 6.22 A\)

 

From the mag. Characteristic

 

\( E_{a} = 282 V\) at 1200 rpm

 

At 1150 rpm, \( E_{a} = 282 \times \frac {1150} {1200} = 270 V\)

 

Terminal voltage \( V_{t} = 270 – 205.6 \times (0.05 + 0.01) = 257.7 V\)
