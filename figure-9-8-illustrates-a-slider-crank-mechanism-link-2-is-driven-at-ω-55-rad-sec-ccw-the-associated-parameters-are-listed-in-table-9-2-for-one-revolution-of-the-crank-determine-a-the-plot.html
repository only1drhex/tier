Figure 9.8 illustrates a slider crank mechanism. Link 2 is driven at ω = 55 rad/sec CCW. The associated parameters are listed in Table 9.2. For one revolution of the crank, determine
(a) the plot of the driving toque
(b) the polar plot of the shaking force
(c) the polar plot of the force at the turning pair between links 2 and 3
(d) the plot of the shaking moment

TABLE 9.2 PARAMETERS OF THE SLIDER CRANK MECHANISM SHOWN IN FIGURE 9.8

\(r_{i} (cm)\) 0 2.5 10 _

\(m (gr)\) _ 2.46 9.84 0
\(ϕ_{i} (degrees)\) _ 0 – _

Solution

(a) Employing Equations (9.3-1) [ A] {x}= {B} – (9.3-5) \((a_{G_{4}})_{x} = \frac{-r_{2} \omega ^{2} \cos (\theta _{3} – \theta _{2}) + r_{3} \dot{\theta} _{3} }{ \cos \theta _{3}} \), the driving torque is shown in Figure 9.9.

(b) Employing Equations (9.3-6)\(\bar{F}_{S} = \bar{F}_{21} + \bar{F}_{41} \) and (9.3-7) \(\left|\bar{F}_{S} \right| = \left\{(F_{21})^{2}_{x} + \left[(F_{21})_{y} + (F_{21})_{y} \right]^{2} \right\}^{1/2}\)
\(\alpha _{S} = \tan 2^{-1} \left\{(F_{21})_{x}, \left[(F_{21})_{y} + (F_{41})_{y} \right] \right\}, -\pi \lt \alpha _{S} \lt \pi \), a polar plot of the shaking force is shown in Figure 9.10.

(c) Employing Equations (9.2-9)\(\left|\bar{F}_{ij} \right| = \left[(F_{ij})^{2}_{x} + (F_{ij})^{2}_{y}\right]^{1/2}\) and (9.2-10)\(\alpha _{ij} = \tan 2^{-1}\left[(F_{ij})_{x} , (F_{ij})_{y}\right], -\pi \lt \alpha _{ij} \lt \pi \) , a polar plot of the force between links 2 and 3 is shown in Figure 9.11.

(d) Employing Equation (9.3-8) \( M_{S} = -M_{21} + (F_{41})_{y} (r_{2} \cos \theta_{2} – r_{3} \cos \theta_{3} )\), the shaking moment is shown in Figure 9.12.
