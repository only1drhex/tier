Solution

With sample 6 deleted, the value of \(\bar{X}\) is \(2.658\), and the value of \(\bar{s}\) is \(0.2354\). The sample size is \(n=5\). From the table, \(A_{3}=1.427\). Therefore the upper control limit is \(2.658+(1.427)(0.2354)=2.994\), and the lower control limit is \(2.658-\) \((1.427)(0.2354)=2.322\).
