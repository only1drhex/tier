Guest Question.

The shaft work in a pump to increase the pressure is small compared to the shaft work in an air compressor for the same pressure increase. Why?

 

Admin
Solution

The reversible work is given by Eq. 7.14 or 7.15 if reversible and no kinetic or potential energy changes

w = −∫ v dP

The liquid has a very small value for v compared to a large value for a gas

……………………………………

Eq.7.14 : \(w=-\int_{i}^{e} v d P+\frac{1}{2}\left( V _{i}^{2}- V _{e}^{2}\right)+g\left(Z_{i}-Z_{e}\right)-\int_{i}^{e} T \delta s_{g e n}\)

Eq.7.15 : \(w=-\int_{i}^{e} v d P\)
