linear trend line . Plot the data on a gragh, and verify visually that a lineae trend line is appropriate . Develop a linear trend equation for the following data . Then use the equation to predict the next two values of the series

Admin
Solution
Period Demand

A plot of the data indicates that a linear trend line is appropriate:

Period Demand y ty
1 44 44 From Table 3.1,with n=9,
2 52 104
3 50 150 \(\sum{t}=45 and \sum{t^2} =285\)
4 54 216
5 55 275
6 55 330
7 60 420
8 56 448
9 \(\underline{62}\) \(\underline{558}\)
488 2,545
\(b=\frac{n\sum{ty}-\sum{t}\sum{y} }{n\sum{t^2}-(\sum{t} )^2 }= \frac{9(2,545)- 45(488)}{9(285)-45(45)}=1.75\)

 

\(a= \frac{\sum{y}-b\sum{t} }{n}= \frac{488 – 1.75(45)}{9}=45.47\)

Thus, the trend equatio is \(F_t=45.47 +1.75t\) The next two forecasts are:

\(F_{10} = 45.47 +1.75(10) =62.97\)

 

\(F_{11} = 45.47 +1.75(11) =64.72\)

You also can use an Excel to obtain the coefficients and a plot . Simply replace the existing data in the template with your data
