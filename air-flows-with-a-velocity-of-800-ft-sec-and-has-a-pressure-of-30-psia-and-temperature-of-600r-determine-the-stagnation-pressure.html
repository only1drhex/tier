Guest Question.

Air flows with a velocity of 800 ft/sec and has a pressure of 30 psia and temperature of
\(600^{\circ}\)R. Determine the stagnation pressure.

Admin
Solution

\(a=(\gamma g_{c}RT)^{1/2}=\left [ (1.4)(32.2)(53.3)(600) \right ]^{1/2}\) = 1201 ft/sec

\(M=\frac{V}{a}=\frac{800}{1201}\) =0.666

\(p_{t}=p\left ( 1+\frac{\gamma -1}{2}M^{2} \right )^{\gamma /\gamma -1}\) = 30\(\left [ 1+\left ( \frac{1.4-1}{2} \right )(0.666)^{2} \right ]^{1.4/(1.4-1)}\)

\(p_{t}=(30)(1+0.0887)^{3.5}\) =(30)(1.346) = 40.4 psia (or 0.279 MPa)
