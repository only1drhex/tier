If the bore in the application had to be 70 mm or larger (selecting a 02-70 mm deep-groove ball bearing), what is the resulting reliability?

Solution

\(R ≈ 1 −\left\{\frac{ [540[\frac{ 1.2(413)}{13 888} ]^{3}− 0.02]}{4.439}\right\} ^{1.483}= 0.999 963\)
