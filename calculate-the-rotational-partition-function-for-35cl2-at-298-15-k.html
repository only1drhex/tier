Guest Question.

Calculate the rotational partition function for \( ^{35}Cl_{2}\) at 298.15 K.

Admin
Solution
\(μ=\frac{0.03497 kg mol^{−1}}{2(6.0221 × 10^{23} mol^{−1})}=2.903 × 10^{−26} kg \)

From Table A.22 in Appendix A, \( r_{e}=1.988 × 10^{−10} m. \)
