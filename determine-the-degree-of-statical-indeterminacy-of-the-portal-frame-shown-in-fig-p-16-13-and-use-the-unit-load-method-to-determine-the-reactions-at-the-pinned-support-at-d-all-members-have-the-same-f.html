Guest Question.

Determine the degree of statical indeterminacy of the portal frame shown in Fig. P.16.13 and use the unit load method to determine the reactions at the pinned support at D. All members have the same flexural rigidity EI. Calculate also the bending moments at A, B and C.
Ans. 11.8 kN (vertical, upwards), 3.3 kN (horizontal to the left).
13.2 kNm at A (anticlockwise), 6.98 kNm at B (clockwise), 9.9 kNm at C (anticlockwise).

Admin
Solution

The frame has a statical indeterminacy of 2 (the vertical and horizontal support reactions at D). Release the frame at D. The vertical reaction, \(R_{1}\), and the horizontal reaction, \(R_{2}\), at D will produce vertical and horizontal displacements \(\Delta_{ DV }\) and \(\Delta_{ DH }\). The equations of compatibility are then

\(\Delta_{ DV }+a_{11} R_{1}+a_{12} R_{2}=0\) (i)

\(\Delta_{ DH }+a_{21} R_{1}+a_{22} R_{2}=0\) (ii)

in which

\(\Delta_{ DV }=\sum \int_{L} \frac{M_{0} M_{1 V }}{E I} d x, \quad \Delta_{ DH }=\sum \int_{L} \frac{M_{0} M_{1 H }}{E I} d x\)

and

\(a_{11}=\sum \int_{L} \frac{M_{1 V ^{2}}}{E I} d x,a_{22}=\sum \int_{L} \frac{M_{1 H ^{2}}}{E I} d x,a_{12}=a_{21}=\sum \int_{L} \frac{M_{1 V } M_{1 H }}{E I} d x\)

Referring now to Fig. S.16.13;

In DC \(M_{0}=0, M_{1 V }=0\) and \(M_{1 H }=-1 x_{1}\)
In CB \(M_{0}=2 x_{2}^{2}, M_{1 V }=-1 x_{2}\) and \(M_{1 H }=-3\)
In BA \(M_{0}=24.5+10 x_{3}, M_{1 V }=-3.5\) and \(M_{1 H }=-1\left(3-x_{3}\right)\)

Then

\(\Delta_{ DV }=\frac{1}{E I}\left[\int_{0}^{3.5}\left(-2 x_{2}^{3}\right) d x_{2}+\int_{0}^{3}-\left(24.5+10 x_{3}\right) 3.5 d x_{3}\right]=\frac{-489.8}{E I}\)

 

\(\Delta_{ DH }=\frac{1}{E I}\left[\int_{0}^{3.5}\left(-6 x_{2}^{2}\right) d x_{2}+\int_{0}^{3}-\left(24.5+10 x_{3}\right)\left(3-x_{3}\right) d x_{3}\right]=\frac{-241.0}{E I}\)

 

\(a_{11}=\frac{1}{E I}\left[\int_{0}^{3.5} x_{2}^{2} d x_{2}+\int_{0}^{3} 3.5^{2} d x_{3}\right]=\frac{51.0}{E I}\)

 

\(a_{22}=\frac{1}{E I}\left[\int_{0}^{3} x_{1}^{2} d x_{1}+\int_{0}^{3.5} 3^{2} d x_{2}+\int_{0}^{3}\left(3-x_{3}\right)^{2} d x_{3}\right]=\frac{49.5}{E I}\)

 

\(a_{12}=a_{21}=\frac{1}{E I}\left[\int_{0}^{3.5} 3 x_{2} d x_{2}+\int_{0}^{3} 3.5\left(3-x_{3}\right) d x_{3}\right]=\frac{34.1}{E I}\)

Substituting these expressions in Eqs (i) and (ii) and solving gives

\(R_{1}=11.8 kN , R_{2}=-3.3 kN\)

Then

\(M_{ A }=11.8 \times 3.5-10 \times 3.0-4 \times 3.5^{2} / 2=-13.2 kNm\) (anticlockwise)

\(M_{ B }=11.8 \times 3.5-3.3 \times 3.0-4 \times 3.5^{2} / 2=6.98 kNm\) (clockwise)

\(M_{ C }=3.3 \times 3.0=9.9 kNm\) (anticlockwise)
