Solution

The fixed rate for DDB with n = 3 is d = 2/3 = 0.6667. Using the half-year convention in year 1 and Equations [16A.14]

\(d_{DB,t}=d\left(1-\sum\limits_{i=1}^{i=t-1}{d_{i}} \right) \) [16A.14]

through [16A.16],

\(d_{SL,n+1}=\frac{1}{2d_{SL,n}}\) [16A.16]

the results are as follows:

\( d_1.\) \( d_{DB,1 }= 0.5d = 0.5(0.6667) = 0.3333\)

\(d_2\) . Cumulative depreciation rate is 0.3333.

\(d_{DB,2}= 0,6667(1 – 0.3333) = 0.4445\) (larger value)
\(d_{SL,2}=\frac{1-0.3333}{3-2+1.5}=0.2267\)

\(d_3\). Cumulative depreciation rate is 0.3333 + 0.4445 = 0.7778.

\(d_{DB,3} = 0.6667(1 – 0.7778) = 0.1481\)

\(d_{SL,2}=\frac{1-0.7778}{3-3+1.5}=0.1487\)

Both values are the same; switch to straight line depreciation.

\(d_4\). This rate is 50% of the last SL rate.

\(d_4 = 0.5(d_{SL,3}) = 0.5(0,1481) = 0.0741\)
