Guest Question.

Select a WT-shape compression member with a length of 20 ft to support a dead load of 5 kips and live load of 15 kips in axial compression. The ends are pinned.

Admin
Solution

Because WT sections are cut from ASTM A992 W-shape beams, the material properties are:

Material Properties:

ASTM A992 \(F_{y}=50 ksi \quad F_{u}=65 ksi\)

Manual
Table 2-3

Calculate the required strength

LRFD ASD
\(P_{u}=1.2(5.00 kips )+1.6(15.0 kips )=30.0 kips\) \(P_{a}=5.00 \text { kips }+15.0 \text { kips }=20.0 \text { kips }\)

\(K=1.0, \text { therefore }(K L)_{x}=(K L)_{y}=20 ft\)
