Guest Question.

Calculate the temperature distribution, temperature at the middle and rate of heat flow at the root of a turbine blade with 80 mm long, 600 mm^2 in cross-section and 150 mm in perimeter. The blade is made of stainless steel (k = 23.3 W/m.K) and is exposed to steam at 1000°C, while its root is maintained at 600°C. The heat transfer coefficient between the blade surface and steam is \(500 W/m^2.K.\)

Admin
Solution

Given : A turbine blade as a fin
\(A_c= 600 mm^2, L = 80 mm,\)
\(P = 150 mm, k = 23.3 W/m.K,\)
\(T_0 = 600°C, T_∞ = 1000°C,\)
\(h = 500 W/m^2.K.\)

To find :
(i) Temperature distribution in the turbine blade.
(ii) Temperature at the middle of blade, and
(iii) The heat flow at root of the blade.

Assumptions :
1. Steady state conditions.
2. Finite long fin with convection heat transfer from its tip.
3. Constant properties.
Analysis : (i) For given turbine blade
\(A_c= 600 mm^2 = 600 × 10^{–6} m^2\)
P = 150 mm = 0.15 m
L = 80 mm = 0.08 m
\(m = \sqrt{\frac{hP}{k A_c}} = \sqrt{\frac{500 \times 0.015}{23.3 × 600 × 10^{-6} }}=73.245\)

mL = 73.245 × 0.08 = 5.86

\(\frac{h}{mk} =\frac{500}{73.245 × 23.3} = 3.662\)
The temperature distribution in finite long fin with convective tip
\(\frac{T – T_∞}{T_0 – T_∞}= \frac{cosh m(L – x) + \frac{h}{mk} sinh m(L – x)}{cosh mL + \frac{h}{mk} sinh m(L – x)}\)

 

\(\frac{T(x) – 1000}{600 – 1000}= \frac{cosh [73.245 \times (0.08 – x)] + 3.662 sinh [73.245 \times (0.08 – x)]}{cosh (5.86) + 3.662 \times sinh (5.86)}\)

 

or \(T(x) – 1000 =\frac{ – 0.400 \times [cosh (5.86 – 73.245 x)+ 3.662 sinh (5.86 – 73.245 x)]}{817.534}\)
It is the required expression for temperature distribution.
(ii) Temperature at the middle of blade i.e.,
x = 40 mm = 0.04 m
Tmiddle = 1000 – 0.489 [cosh (5.86 – 73.245 × 0.04) + 3.662 sinh (5.86 – 73.245 × 0.04)]
= 1000 – 0.489 [43.591] = 978.68°C.
(iii) The heat transfer rate from fin
\(Q_{fin} = \sqrt{hPk A_c} (T_0 – T_∞) × \frac{sinh mL + \frac{h}{mk} cosh mL }{cosh mL + 3.662 \times sinh mL}\)

 

\(=\sqrt{500 ×0.15 ×23.3 ×600×10^{-6} } (600 – 1000) × \frac{sinh (5.86) + 3.662 \times cosh (5.86) }{cosh (5.86) + \frac{h}{mk}sinh (5.86)}\)

= – 409.58 W.
(Heat transfer towards centre)
