Guest Question.

Calcuate the partition function and the molar energy, enthalpy, and entropy and constant pressure heat capacity 1.000 mol of \( ^{35}Cl_{2}\) at 298.15K and the standard-state pressure \( P°.\) Assume that the uncorrected harmonic oscillator–rigid rotor energy levels can be used.

Data: \( \widetilde{v}_{e}=559.7 cm^{−1}\)

 

\( \widetilde{B}_{e}=0.2440 cm^{−1}\)

 

\( M=0.0699375 kg mol^{−1}\)

 

\( m=1.16134×10^{−25} kg\)

 

\( V=0.02479m^{3}\) (the molar volume of an ideal gas at \(P°)\)

Admin
Solution
\(Z_{tr}=\Bigl(\frac{2πmk_{B}T}{h^{2}}\Bigr)^{3/2} V\)

 

\(=\Bigl(\frac{2π(1.16134 × 10^{−25} kg)(1.3807 × 10^{−23} J K^{−1})(298.15K)}{(6.6261 × 10^{−34} J s)^{2}}\Bigr) ^{3/2}(0.02479 m^{3})\)

 

\(=1.4028 × 10^{31}\)

 

\(\frac{Z_{tr}}{N}=\frac{ 1.4028 × 10^{31}}{6.022 × 10^{23}}= 2.329 × 10^{7}\)

 

The rotational partition function was already calculated in :

\(Z_{rot}= 424.7\)

 

The vibrational partition function was already calculated in :

\(Z_{vib}= 1.072\)

 

\(Z_{el} ≈ 1.000\)

 

\(Z= Z_{tr}Z_{rot}Z_{vib}Z_{el}= (1.4028 × 10^{31})(424.7)(1.072)(1.000) =6.3867 × 10^{33}\)

 

\(\frac{Z}{N}=\frac{6.3867 × 10^{33}}{6.02214 × 10^{23}}=1.0605 × 10^{10}\)

 

\(U^{°}_{m,tr}= \frac{3}{2}RT =(1.500 mol)(8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1})(298.15K)= 3718.45 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(U^{°}_{m,rot} =RT= (1.000 mol)(8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1})(298.15K) =2478.97 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(U^{°}_{m,vib} =\frac{N_{Av}hν}{e^{hν/k_{B}T }− 1}=\frac{ N_{Av}hc\widetilde{v}}{e^{hc \widetilde{v}/k_{B}T} – 1}=481.92 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(U^{°}_{m,el}≈ 0\)

 

\(U^{°}_{m}= 3718.45 J mol^{−1} + 2478.97 J mol^{−1} + 481.92 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(=6679.34.42 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(=6.67934 kJ mol^{−1}\)

 

\(H^{°}_{m}= U^{°}_{m}+ PV_{m} =U^{°}_{m} + RT =6679.34 J mol^{−1} + 2478.97 J mol^{−1}\)

 

\(=9158.31 J mol^{−1}= 9.15831 kJ mol^{−1}\)

 

These values are relative to the ground-state energy. The experimental value of \(H^{°}_{m} is 9.181 kJ mol^{−1} \) relative to this zero of energy.

\(S^{°}_{m,tr}= (8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1})\biggl(9.2100 + \frac{5}{2} \ln(298.15) + \frac{3}{2} \ln(0.0699375) + \ln(1) \biggr) \)

 

\(=161.831 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(S^{°}_{m,rot}=\frac{U_{m, rot}}{T} + R \ln(Z_{rot}) = R \ln(Z_{rot})\)

 

\(=8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1} + (8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}) \ln(424.7)= 58.627 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(S^{°}_{m,vib}=\frac{U_{m,vib}}{T}+ R \ln(Z_{vib})\)

 

\(=\frac{481.92 J mol^{−1}}{298.15K}+ (8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}) \ln(1.072) =2.194 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(S^{°}_{m,el}=\frac{U^{°}_{m,el}}{T}+ R \ln(Z_{el}) ≈ 0 + 0 = 0\)

 

\(S^{°}_{m} =161.831 J K^{−1} mol^{−1} + 58.627 J K^{−1} mol^{−1} + 2.194 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(=222.652 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

The experimental value is \(223.079 JK^{−1} mol^{−1}.\)

 

\(C^{°}_{V,m,tr} =\frac{3}{2}R =12.4718 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(C^{°}_{V,m,rot} =R= 8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

\(C^{°}_{V,m,vib} =R\Bigl(\frac{hν}{k_{B}T} \Bigr)^{2} \frac{e^{hν/k_{B}T}}{(e^{hν/k_{B}T} − 1)^{2}} =Rx^{2} \frac{e^{x}}{(e^{x} − 1)^{2}} =4.680 J K^{-1} mol^{-1}\)

 

\(C^{°}_{V,mel} ≈ 0\)

 

\(C^{°}_{V,m} =25.466 J K^{-1} mol^{-1}\)

 

\(C^{°}_{P,m} =25.466 J K^{−1} mol^{−1} + 8.3145 J K^{−1} mol^{−1} = 33.780 J K^{−1} mol^{−1}\)

 

The experimental value is \( 33.949 JK^{−1} mol^{−1}\) at this temperature. The discrepancies between our values and the experimental values are presumably due to the inadequacy of the harmonic oscillator–rigid rotor energy level expression.
