Guest Question.

Determine the support reactions and the forces in the members of the space truss shown in Fig. 6.20.

Admin
Solution

The truss consists of j = 4 joints, m = 6 members and there are r = 6 support reactions. Therefore, the necessary condition for statical determinacy is satisfied:

f = 3 j − (m + r) = 12 − (6 + 6) = 0.

The equilibrium conditions for the complete truss

\(\sum{F_{x}} =0 : A_{x} =0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{y}} =0 : A_{y}+B_{y} +F =0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{z}} =0 : A_{z}+B_{z} +C_{z} =0\) ,

\(\sum{M_{x}} =0 : -a F +a B_{z} =0\) ,

\(\sum{M_{y}} =0 : aC_{z} -a A_{z} =0\) ,

\(\sum{M_{z}} =0 : aA_{y} =0\)

yield the support reactions:

\(\underline{\underline{A_{x} =0} } , \underline{\underline{A_{y} =0} } , \underline{\underline{A_{z}=-\frac{1}{2} F} }\) ,

\(\underline{\underline{B_{y}= -F} } , \underline{\underline{B_{z} =F} } , \underline{\underline{C_{z} =-\frac{1}{2} F} }\) .

In order to determine the forces in the members we apply the method of joints. Note that (with the exception of bar 4) all the members have the angle \(45^{\circ }\) with respect to the corresponding coordinate axes. Thus, equilibrium at the joints I and II leads to

I \(\sum{F_{x}} =0 : \frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{1} -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{3} =0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{y}} =0 : \frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{2} +F =0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{z}} =0 : -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{1} -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{2} -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{3} =0\) ,

\(\rightarrow \underline{\underline{S_{1} =S_{3} =\frac{\sqrt{2} }{2} F} } , \underline{\underline{S_{2}=-\sqrt{2}F } }\) .

II \(\sum{F_{x}} =0 : A_{x} -\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{1} -S_{4}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{5}=0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{y}} =0 : A_{y} +\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{5} =0\) ,

\(\rightarrow \underline{\underline{S_{4}=-\frac{1}{2} F} } , \underline{\underline{S_{5} =0} }\) .

From symmetry we find

\(\underline{\underline{S_{6}=S_{5}=0} }\) .

As a check we write down the equilibrium conditions for joint IV:

\(\sum{F_{x}} =0 : \frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{6} +S_{4} +\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{3} =0 \rightarrow 0-\frac{F}{2} +\frac{F}{2}=0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{y}} =0 : \frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{6} =0\) ,

\(\sum{F_{z}} =0 : C_{z}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} } S_{3} =0 \rightarrow -\frac{F}{2}+ \frac{F}{2} =0\) .
