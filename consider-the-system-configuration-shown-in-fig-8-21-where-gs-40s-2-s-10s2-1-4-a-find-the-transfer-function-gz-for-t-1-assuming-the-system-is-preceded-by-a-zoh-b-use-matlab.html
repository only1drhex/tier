Guest Question.

Consider the system configuration shown in Fig. 8.21, where

\(G(s)=\frac{40(s+2)}{(s+10)\left(s^{2}-1.4\right)}\).

(a) Find the transfer function G(z) for T = 1 assuming the system is preceded by a ZOH.

(b) Use MATLAB to draw the root locus of the system with respect to K.

(c) What is the range of K for which the closed-loop system is stable?

(d) Compare your results of part (c) to the case where an analog controller is used (that is, where the sampling switch is always closed). Which system has a larger allowable value of K?

(e) Use MATLAB to compute the step response of both the continuous and discrete systems with K chosen to yield a damping factor of ζ = 0.5 for the continuous case.

Admin
Solution

(a) Using partial fraction expansion along with Table 8.1,

TABLE 8.1:

Laplace Transforms and z-Transforms of Simple Discrete-Time Functions
No. F(s) f(kT) F(z)
1 \(1, k=0 ; 0, k \neq 0\) 1
2 \(1, k=k_{o} ; 0, k \neq k_{o}\) \(\mathcal z^{-k_{o}}\)
3 \(\frac{1}{s}\) 1(kT) \(\frac{z}{z-1}\)
4 \(\frac{1}{s^{2}}\) kT \(\frac{T z}{(z-1)^{2}}\)
5 \(\frac{1}{s^{3}}\) \(\frac{1}{2 !}(k T)^{2}\) \(\frac{T^{2}}{2}\left[\frac{z(z+1)}{(z-1)^{3}}\right]\)
6 \(\frac{1}{s^{4}}\) \(\frac{1}{3 !}(k T)^{3}\) \(\frac{T^{3}}{6}\left[\frac{z\left(z^{2}+4 z+1\right)}{(z-1)^{4}}\right]\)
7 \(\frac{1}{s^{m}}\) \(\lim _{a \rightarrow 0} \frac{(-1)^{m-1}}{(m-1) !}\left(\frac{\partial^{m-1}}{\partial a^{m-1}} e^{-a k T}\right)\) \(\lim _{a \rightarrow 0} \frac{(-1)^{m-1}}{(m-1) !}\left(\frac{\partial^{m-1}}{\partial a^{m-1}} \frac{z}{z-e^{-a T}}\right)\)
8 \(\frac{1}{s+a}\) \(e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{z}{z-e^{-a T}}\)
9 \(\frac{1}{(s+a)^{2}}\) \(k T e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{T z e^{-a T}}{\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)^{2}}\)
10 \(\frac{1}{(s+a)^{3}}\) \(\frac{1}{2}(k T)^{2} e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{T^{2}}{2} e^{-a T} z \frac{\left(z+e^{-a T}\right)}{\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)^{3}}\)
11 \(\frac{1}{(s+a)^{m}}\) \(\frac{(-1)^{m-1}}{(m-1) !}\left(\frac{\partial^{m-1}}{\partial a^{m-1}} e^{-a k T}\right)\) \(\frac{(-1)^{m-1}}{(m-1) !}\left(\frac{\partial^{m-1}}{\partial a^{m-1}} \frac{z}{z-e^{-a T}}\right)\)
12 \(\frac{a}{s(s+a)}\) \(1-e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{z\left(1-e^{-a T}\right)}{(z-1)\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)}\)
13 \(\frac{a}{s^{2}(s+a)}\) \(\frac{1}{a}\left(a k T-1+e^{-a k T}\right)\) \(\frac{z\left[\left(a T-1+e^{-a T}\right) z+\left(1-e^{-a T}-a T e^{-a T}\right)\right]}{a(z-1)^{2}\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)}\)
14 \(\frac{b-a}{(s+a)(s+b)}\) \(e^{-a k T}-e^{-b k T}\) \(\frac{\left(e^{-a T}-e^{-b T}\right) z}{\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)\left(z-e^{-b T}\right)}\)
15 \(\frac{s}{(s+a)^{2}}\) \((1-a k T) e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{z\left[z-e^{-a T}(1+a T)\right]}{\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)^{2}}\)
16 \(\frac{a^{2}}{s(s+a)^{2}}\) \(1-e^{-a k T}(1+a k T)\) \(\frac{z\left[z\left(1-e^{-a T}-a T e^{-a T}\right)+e^{-2 a T}-e^{-a T}+a T e^{-a T}\right]}{(z-1)\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)^{2}}\)
17 \(\frac{(b-a) s}{(s+a)(s+b)}\) \(b e^{-b k T}-a e^{-a k T}\) \(\frac{z\left[z(b-a)-\left(b e^{-a T}-a e^{-b T}\right)\right]}{\left(z-e^{-a T}\right)\left(z-e^{-b T}\right)}\)
18 \(\frac{a}{s^{2}+a^{2}}\) \(\sin a k T\) \(\frac{z \sin a T}{z^{2}-(2 \cos a T) z+1}\)
19 \(\frac{s}{s^{2}+a^{2}}\) \(\cos a k T\) \(\frac{z(z-\cos a T)}{z^{2}-(2 \cos a T) z+1}\)
20 \(\frac{s+a}{(s+a)^{2}+b^{2}}\) \(e^{-a k T} \cos b k T\) \(\frac{z\left(z-e^{-a T} \cos b T\right)}{z^{2}-2 e^{-a T}(\cos b T) z+e^{-2 a T}}\)
21 \(\frac{b}{(s+a)^{2}+b^{2}}\) \(e^{-a k T} \sin b k T\) \(\frac{z e^{-a T} \sin b T}{z^{2}-2 e^{-a T}(\cos b T) z+e^{-2 a T}}\)
22 \(\frac{a^{2}+b^{2}}{s\left[(s+a)^{2}+b^{2}\right]}\) \(1-e^{-a k T}\left(\cos b k T+\frac{a}{b} \sin b k T\right)\) \(\frac{z(A z+B)}{(z-1)\left[z^{2}-2 e^{-a T}(\cos b T) z+e^{-2 a T}\right]}\)

 

\(A=1-e^{-a T} \cos b T-\frac{a}{b} e^{-a T} \sin b T\)

 

\(B=e^{-2 a T}+\frac{a}{b} e^{-a T} \sin b T-e^{-a T} \cos b T\)

 

\(G(z)=\frac{z-1}{z} \mathcal Z \left\{\frac{G(s)}{s}\right\}=\frac{z-1}{z} \mathcal Z \left\{\frac{40(s+2)}{s(s+10)\left(s^{2}-1.4\right)}\right\}\)

= \(\frac{z-1}{z} \mathcal Z \left\{40\left(-\frac{0.1429}{s}+\frac{0.0081}{s+10}+\frac{0.0331}{s+\sqrt{1.4}}+\frac{0.1017}{s-\sqrt{1.4}}\right)\right\}\)

= \(\frac{z-1}{z} \mathcal Z \left\{4 0 \left(-0.1429 \frac{z}{z-1}+0.0081 \frac{z}{z-e^{-10}}\right.\right. \left.\left.+0.0331 \frac{z}{z-e^{-\sqrt{1.4}}}+0.1017 \frac{z}{z-e^{\sqrt{1.4}}}\right)\right\}\)

= \(\frac{7.967 z^{-1}+1.335 z^{-2}-0.3245 z^{-3}}{1-3.571 z^{-1}+1.000 z^{-2}-0.00004540 z^{-3}}\)

Alternately, we could compute the same result using c2d inMATLAB with G(s).

(b) The z-plane root locus is shown.

(c) A portion of the locus is outside the unit circle for any value of K; therefore, the closed-loop system for the discrete case is unstable for all K.

(d) The s-plane root locus is shown. The closed-loop system is stable for K > 0.175. The analog case has a larger allowable K.

(e) Since ζ = 0.5 must be achieved, an analytical approach would be to let a desired closed-loop pole be :

\(s_{d}=\sigma+\sqrt{3} \sigma j\)

Evaluate the continuous characteristic equation at \(s_{d}\):

\(\left.\left\{1+\frac{40 K(s+2)}{s(s+10)\left(s^{2}-1.4\right)}\right\}\right|_{s=\sigma+\sqrt{3}\sigma j}=0\)

and find that a cubic results, i.e., there are three places on the locus where ζ = 0.5.

\(\Longrightarrow\left(-8 \sigma^{3}-20 \sigma^{2}+40 K \sigma-1.4 \sigma+80 K-14\right)\)

 

\(+\left(34.641 \sigma^{2}+40 \sqrt{3} K-1.4 \sqrt{3}\right) j=0 \)

 

\(\Longrightarrow \sigma=\left[\begin{array}{l} -3.7732 \\ -0.6857 \\ -0.5411\end{array}\right], \quad K=\left[\begin{array}{l} 1.9216 \\ 0.3778 \\ 0.3056\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\Longrightarrow s=\left[\begin{array}{l} -3.7732-6.5354 j \\ -0.6857-1.1876 j \\ -0.5411-0.9373 j\end{array}\right]\)

 

\(\Longrightarrow \omega_{n}=\left[\begin{array}{l} 7.5456 \\ 1.3713 \\ 1.0823\end{array}\right], \quad \zeta=0.5\)

Any of these gains yield a damping factor of ζ= 0.5 for the continuous case; however, we will use the lowest value of K. Alternatively, we could use rlocfind from MATLAB to determine K at the desired ζ = 0.5
