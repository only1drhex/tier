Guest Question.

Fig. P.18.12 shows a portal frame which is pinned to its foundation and which carries vertical and horizontal loads as shown. If the relative values of the plastic moments of resistance are those given determine the relationship between the load W and the plastic moment parameter \(M_{ P }\). Calculate also the foundation reactions at collapse.
Ans. W = 0.3P. Horizontal: 0.44W at A, 0.56W at G. Vertical: 0.89W at A, 2.11W at G.

Admin
Solution

For this frame there are three possible beam mechanisms as shown in Figs S.18.12(a), (b) and (c) and one sway mechanism, Fig. S.18.12(d).
For (a)

\(W(3 \theta+2 \theta+\theta)=M_{ P }\left(2 \theta+\frac{8 \theta}{3}+\frac{\theta}{3}\right)\)

which gives

\(W=0.833 M_{ P }\)

For (b)

\(W(3 \theta+6 \theta+3 \theta)=M_{ P }(2 \theta+4 \theta+\theta)\)

i.e.

\(W=0.583 M_{ P }\)

For (c)

\(W(3 \theta+6 \theta+9 \theta)=M_{ P }(2 \theta+8 \theta+3 \theta)\)

so that

\(W=0.722 M_{ P }\)

For (d)

\(W \times 9 \theta=M_{ P }\left(2 \theta+\frac{3 \theta}{2}\right)\)

i.e.

\(W=0.389 M_{ P }\)

For (a) + (d) with a hinge cancellation at B

\(W(6 \theta+9 \theta)=M_{ P }\left(\frac{8 \theta}{3}+\frac{\theta}{3}+\frac{3 \theta}{2}\right)\)

so that

\(W=0.3 M_{ P }\)

For (b) + (d) with a hinge cancellation at B

\(W(12 \theta+9 \theta)=M_{ P }\left(4 \theta+\theta+\frac{3 \theta}{2}\right)\)

i.e.

\(W=0.309 M_{ P }\)

For (c) + (d) with a hinge cancellation at B

\(W(18 \theta+9 \theta)=M_{ P }\left(8 \theta+3 \theta+\frac{3 \theta}{2}\right)\)

which gives

\(W=0.463 M_{ P }\)

The minimum value of W to cause collapse is therefore 0.3 \(M_{ P }\) with plastic hinges at C and F.
Referring to Fig. S.18.12(e)

\(6 R_{ G , H }=M_{ P }=\frac{W}{0.3}\)

i.e.

\(R_{ G , H }=0.56 W\)

Resolving horizontally

\(R_{ A , H }=W-0.56 W =0.44 W\)

Taking moments about A

\(R_{ G , V } \times 12+0.56 W \times 3=W(3+6+9+9)\)

i.e.

\(R_{ G , V }=2.11 W\)

Resolving vertically

\(R_{ A , V }=3 W -2.11 W =0.89 W\).
