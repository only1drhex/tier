Guest Question.

A vertical retaining wall supports \(10 \mathrm{~m}\) of a granular backfill having the following properties: \(\phi^{\prime}=28^{\circ}\) and \(\gamma=18 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{3}\) (Fig. 11.12). The water table is at a depth of \(3 \mathrm{~m}\) from the horizontal backfill. The saturated unit weight below the water table is \(\gamma_{s a t}=19.5 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{3}\). Determine the total active earth thrust and its point of application.

Admin
Solution

Consider active pressure distribution:

\(K_{a}=\frac{1-\sin \phi^{\prime}}{1+\sin \phi^{\prime}}=\frac{1-\sin 28^{\circ}}{1+\sin 28^{\circ}}=0.36\)

At \(z=0\)

\(p_{a}=K_{a} \gamma z=0\)

At \(z=3 \mathrm{~m}\),

\(p_{a}=K_{a} \gamma z=0.36 \times 18 \times 3=19.44 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{2}\)

At \(z=10 \mathrm{~m}\),

\(p_{a}=K_{a} \gamma z=0.36 \times(19.5-9.81) \times 10=34.9 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{2}\)

Water pressure distribution:

At \(z=3 \mathrm{~m}\)

\(p_{w}=\gamma_{w} z=0\)

At \(z=10 \mathrm{~m}\)

\(p_{w}=\gamma_{w} z=9.81 \times 7=68.67 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{2}\)

Total active earth thrust is the sum of the active and water pressure distribution diagrams (see Fig.11.13).

\(\begin{aligned}&P_{a}=0.5 \times 19.44 \times 3+19.44 \times 7+0.5 \times 24.46 \times 7+0.5 \times 68.67 \times 7=491.2 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m} \\ &\bar{z}=\frac{29.1 \times 8+136.1 \times 3.5+85.61 \times 2.3+240.34 \times 2.3}{491.2}=2.96 \mathrm{~m}\end{aligned}\)
