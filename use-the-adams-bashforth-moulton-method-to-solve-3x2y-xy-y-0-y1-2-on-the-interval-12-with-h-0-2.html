Guest Question.

Use the Adams-Bashforth-Moulton method to solve \(3 x^{2} y^{\prime \prime}-x y^{\prime}+y=0 ; y(1)=4, y^{\prime}(1)=2\) on the interval [1,2] with h = 0.2.

Admin
Solution

It follows from , tha \(f(x, y, z)=z, g(x, y, z)=(x z-y) /\left(3 x^{2}\right), x_{0}=1, y_{0}=4, \text { and } z_{0}=2\). From Table 20-5, we have

\(\begin{array}{lll}x_{1}=1.2 & y_{1}=4.3879715 & z_{1}=1.8855447 \\x_{2}=1.4 & y_{2}=4.7560600 & z_{2}=1.7990579 \\x_{3}=1.6 & y_{3}=5.1088123 & z_{3}=1.7309980\end{array}\)

Using (20.6), we compute

\(\begin{array}{ll}y_{0}^{\prime}=z_{0}=2 & y_{1}^{\prime}=z_{1}=1.8855447 \\y_{2}^{\prime}=z_{2}=1.7990579 &y_{3}^{\prime}=z_{3}=1.7309980\end{array}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&z_{0}^{\prime}=\frac{x_{0} z_{0}-y_{0}}{3 x_{0}^{2}}=\frac{1(2)-4}{3(1)^{2}}=-0.6666667 \\&z_{1}^{\prime}=\frac{x_{1} z_{1}-y_{1}}{3 x_{1}^{2}}=\frac{1.2(1.8855447)-4.3879715}{3(1.2)^{2}}=-0.4919717 \\&z_{2}^{\prime}=\frac{x_{2} z_{2}-y_{2}}{3 x_{2}^{2}}=\frac{1.4(1.7990579)-4.7560600}{3(1.4)^{2}}=-0.3805066 \\&z_{3}^{\prime}=\frac{x_{3} z_{3}-y_{3}}{3 x_{3}^{2}}=\frac{1.6(1.7309980)-5.1088123}{3(1.6)^{2}}=-0.3045854\end{aligned}\)

Then using (20.5), we compute

\(\begin{aligned}n=3: \quad x_{4}=& 1.8 \\p y_{4}=& y_{3}+\frac{h}{24}\left(55 y_{3}^{\prime}-59 y_{2}^{\prime}+37 y_{1}^{\prime}-9 y_{0}^{\prime}\right) \\=& 5.1088123+(0.2 / 24)[55(1.7309980)-59(1.7990579)+37(1.8855447)-9(2)]=5.4490260 \\p z_{4}=& z_{3}+\frac{h}{24}\left(55 z_{3}^{\prime}-59 z_{2}^{\prime}+37 z_{1}^{\prime}-9 z_{0}^{\prime}\right) \\=& 1.7309980+(0.2 / 24)[55(-0.3045854)-59(-0.3805066)+37(-0.4919717)\\&-9(-0.6666667)]=1.6767876 \\p y_{4}^{\prime}=& p z_{4}=1.6767876 \\p z_{4}^{\prime}=& \frac{x_{4} p z_{4}-p y_{4}}{3 x_{4}^{2}}=\frac{1.8(1.6767876)-5.4490260}{3\left(1.8^{2}\right)}=-0.2500832 \\y_{4}=& y_{3}+\frac{h}{24}\left(9 p y_{4}^{\prime}+19 y_{3}^{\prime}-5 y_{2}^{\prime}+y_{1}^{\prime}\right) \\=& 5.1088123+(0.2 / 24)[9(1.6767876)+19(1.7309980)-5(1.7990579)+1.8855447] \\=& 5.4493982\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}z_{4} &=z_{3}+\frac{h}{24}\left(9 p z_{4}^{\prime}+19 z_{3}^{\prime}-5 z_{2}^{\prime}+z_{1}^{\prime}\right) \\&=1.7309980+(0.2 / 24)[9(-0.2500832)+19(-0.3045854)-5(-0.3805066)+(-0.4919717)] \\&=1.6757705 \\y_{4}^{\prime} &=z_{4}=1.6757705 \\z_{4}^{\prime} &=\frac{x_{4} z_{4}-y_{4}}{3 x_{4}^{2}}=\frac{1.8(1.6757705)-5.4493982}{3\left(1.8^{2}\right)}=-0.2503098\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}n=4: \quad x_{5} &=2.0 \\p y_{5} &=y_{4}+\frac{h}{24}\left(55 y_{4}^{\prime}-59 y_{3}^{\prime}+37 y_{2}^{\prime}-9 y_{1}^{\prime}\right) \\&=5.4493982+(0.2 / 24)[55(1.6757705)-59(1.7309980)+37(1.7990579)-9(1.8855447)] \\&=5.7796793 \\p z_{5} &=z_{4}+\frac{h}{24}\left(55 z_{4}^{\prime}-59 z_{3}^{\prime}+37 z_{2}^{\prime}-9 z_{1}^{\prime}\right) \\&=1.6757705+(0.2 / 24)[55(-0.2503098)-59(-0.3045854)+37(-0.3805066)-9(-0.4919717)] \\&=1.6303746 \\p y_{5}^{\prime} &=p z_{5}=1.6303746 \\p z_{5}^{\prime} &=\frac{x_{5} p z_{5}-p y_{5}}{3 x_{5}^{2}}=\frac{2.0(1.6303746)-5.7796793}{3(2.0)^{2}}=-0.2099108 \\y_{5} &=y_{4}+\frac{h}{24}\left(9 p y_{5}^{\prime}+19 y_{4}^{\prime}-5 y_{3}^{\prime}+y_{2}^{\prime}\right) \\&=5.4493982+(0.2 / 24)[9(1.6303746)+19(1.6757705)-5(1.7309980)+1.7990579] \\&=5.7798739\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}z_{5} &=z_{4}+\frac{h}{24}\left(9 p z_{5}^{\prime}+19 z_{4}^{\prime}-5 z_{3}^{\prime}+z_{2}^{\prime}\right) \\&=1.6757705+(0.2 / 24)[9(-0.2099108)+19(-0.2503098)-5(-0.3045854)+(-0.3805066)] \\&=1.6299149 \\y_{5}^{\prime} &=z_{5}=1.6299149 \\z_{5}^{\prime} &=\frac{x_{5} z_{5}-y_{5}}{3 x_{5}^{2}}=\frac{2.0(1.6299149)-5.7798739}{3(2.0)^{2}}=-0.2100037\end{aligned}\)
