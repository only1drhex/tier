Guest Question.

Select a WT-shape compression member with a length of 20 ft to support a dead load of 20 kips and live load of 60 kips in axial compression. The ends are pinned.

Admin
Solution

Because WT sections are cut from ASTM A992 W-shape beams, the material properties are:

Material Properties:

ASTM A992 \(F_{y}=50 ksi \quad F_{u}=65 ksi\)
