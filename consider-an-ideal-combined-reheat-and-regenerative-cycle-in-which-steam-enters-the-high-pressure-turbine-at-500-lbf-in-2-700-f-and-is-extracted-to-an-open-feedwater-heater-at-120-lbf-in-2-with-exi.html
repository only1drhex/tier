Guest Question.

Consider an ideal combined reheat and regenerative cycle in which steam enters the high-pressure turbine at 500 \(lbf / in .^{2}\), 700 F, and is extracted to an open feedwater heater at 120 \(lbf / in .^{2}\) with exit as saturated liquid. The remainder of the steam is reheated to 700 F at this pressure, 120 \(lbf / in .^{2}\), and is fed to the low-pressure turbine. The condenser pressure is 2.225 \(lbf / in .^{2}\). Calculate the thermal efficiency of the cycle and the net work per pound-mass of steam.

Admin
Solution

5: \(h _{5}=1356.66, s _{5}=1.6112\)

7: \(h _{7}=1378.17, \quad s _{7}=1.7825\)

3: \(h _{3}= h _{ f }=312.59, v _{3}=0.01788\)

C.V. T1

\(\begin{aligned}s _{5} &= s _{6} \quad \Rightarrow h _{6}=1209.76 \\w _{ T1 } &= h _{5}- h _{6}=1356.66-1209.76 \\&=146.9 Btu / lbm\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Pump 1

\(\begin{aligned}&\begin{aligned}w _{ P 1} &= h _{2}- h _{1}= v _{1}\left( P _{2}- P _{1}\right) \\&=0.01623(120-2)=0.354 \\\Rightarrow \quad & h _{2}= h _{1}+ w _{ P 1}=93.73+0.354=94.08 Btu / lbm\end{aligned}\end{aligned}\)

C.V. FWH

\(\begin{aligned}& x h _{6}+(1- x ) h _{2}= h _{3} \\& x =\frac{ h _{3}- h _{2}}{ h _{6}- h _{2}}=\frac{312.59-94.08}{1209.76-94.08}=0.1958\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Pump 2

\(\begin{aligned}& w _{ P 2}= h _{4}- h _{3}= v _{3}\left( P _{4}- P _{3}\right)=0.01788(500-120)(144 / 778)=1.26 Btu / lbm \\&\Rightarrow h _{4}= h _{3}+ w _{ P 2}=312.59+1.26=313.85 Btu / lbm \\& q _{ H }= h _{5}- h _{4}+(1- x )\left( h _{7}- h _{6}\right)=1042.81+135.43=1178.2 Btu / lbm\end{aligned}\)

C.V. Turbine 2

\(\begin{aligned}& s _{7}= s _{8} \Rightarrow x _{8}=(1.7825-0.1817) / 1.7292=0.9257 \\& h _{8}= h _{ f }+ x _{8} h _{ fg }=97.97+0.9257 \times 1019.78=1041.98 \\& w _{ T 2}= h _{7}- h _{8}=1378.17-1041.98=336.19 \\& w _{ net }= w _{ T1 }+(1- x ) w _{ T 2}-(1- x ) w _{ P 1}- w _{ P 2} \\&=146.9+270.36-0.285-1.26= 4 1 5 . 7 2 kJ / kg \\&\eta_{\text {cycle }}= w _{\text {net }} / q _{ H }=415.72 / 1178.2= 0 . 3 5 3\end{aligned}\)
