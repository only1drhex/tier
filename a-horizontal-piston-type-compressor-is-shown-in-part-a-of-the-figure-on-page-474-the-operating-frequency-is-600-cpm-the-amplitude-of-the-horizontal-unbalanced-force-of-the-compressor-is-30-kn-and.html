Guest Question.

A horizontal piston-type compressor is shown in part (a) of the figure on page 474. The operating frequency is 600 cpm. The amplitude of the horizontal unbalanced force of the compressor is 30 kN, and it creates a rocking motion of the foundation about point O (see part (b) on page 474). The mass moment of inertia of the compressor assembly about the axis \(b^{\prime} O b^{\prime} \text { is } 16 \times 10^{5} kg - m ^{2}\) (see part (c) on page 474).

Determine:

a. the resonant frequency

b. the amplitude of rocking at resonance

Admin
Solution

The moment of inertia of the foundation block and the compressor assembly about \(b^{\prime} O b^{\prime}:\)

The moment of inertia of the foundation block about the axis through its centroid = \(\frac{1}{12} m\left(L^{2}+h^{2}\right)\). Therefore, the moment of inertia about the axis through:

 

\(O=\frac{1}{12} m\left(L^{2}+h^{2}\right)+m\left(\frac{h}{2}\right)^{2}=\frac{1}{12} m L^{2}+\frac{1}{3} m h^{2}=\frac{m}{3}\left[\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}+h^{2}\right]\)

 

\(I_{0}=\left(\frac{W_{\text {foundation block }}}{3 g}\right)\left[\left(\frac{L}{2}\right)^{2}+h^{2}\right]+16 \times 10^{5} kg – m ^{2}\)

 

Assume the unit weight of concrete is \(23.58 kN / m ^{3}:\)

 

\(W_{\text {foundation block }}=(8 \times 6 \times 3)(23.58)=3395.52 kN =3395.52 \times 10^{3} N\)

 

\(I_{0}=\frac{3395.52 \times 10^{3}}{(3)(9.81)}\left(3^{2}+3^{2}\right)+16 \times 10^{5}=36.768 \times 10^{5} kg – m ^{2}\)

 

Equivalent radius of the foundation: From Equation 16.50, the equivalent radius is:

 

\(r_{0}=\sqrt[4]{\frac{B L^{3}}{3 \pi}}\) (16.50)

 

\(r_{0}=\sqrt[4]{\frac{B L^{3}}{3 \pi}}=\sqrt[4]{\frac{8 \times 6^{3}}{3 \pi}}=3.67 m\)

 

a. Resonant frequency:

 

\(k_{\theta}=\frac{8 G r_{0}^{3}}{3(1-v)}=\frac{(8)(18,000)(3.67)^{3}}{(3)(1-0.35)}=3650279 kN – m / rad\)

 

\(B_{\theta}=\frac{3(1-v)}{8} \frac{I_{0}}{\rho r_{0}^{5}}=\frac{3(1-0.35)}{8} \frac{36.768 \times 10^{5}}{1800(3.67)^{5}}=0.748\)

 

\(D_{\theta}=\frac{0.15}{\sqrt{B_{\theta}}\left(1+B_{\theta}\right)}=\frac{0.15}{\sqrt{0.748}(1+0.748)}=0.099\)

 

From Equation 16.51:

 

\(f_{m}=\left(\frac{1}{2 \pi} \sqrt{\frac{k_{\theta}}{I_{0}}}\right)\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-2 D_{\theta}^{2}}}\right)\) (16.51)

 

\(\begin{aligned}f_{n} &=\left(\frac{1}{2 \pi} \sqrt{\frac{k_{\theta}}{I_{0}}}\right)\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-2 D_{\theta}^{2}}}\right) \\&=\left(\frac{1}{2 \pi} \sqrt{\frac{3650279 \times 10^{3} N – m / rad }{36.768 \times 10^{5}}}\right)\left[\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-2(0.099)^{2}}}\right] \\&=5.05 cps =303 cpm\end{aligned}\)

 

b. Amplitude of vibration at resonance:

 

\(M_{y \text { (operating frequency) }}=\text { unbalanced force } \times 4=30 \times 4=120 kN – m\)

 

\(M_{y \text { (at resonance) }}=120\left(\frac{f_{m}}{f_{\text {operating }}}\right)^{2}=120\left(\frac{303}{600}\right)^{2}=30.6 kN – m\)

 

\(\left(m_{1} e \omega^{2}\right) z^{\prime}=M_{y}\)

 

\(\omega_{\text {resonance }}=\frac{(2 \pi)(303)}{60}=31.73 rad / s\)

 

\(m_{1} e z^{\prime}=\frac{M_{y}}{\omega^{2}}=\frac{30.6 \times 10^{3} N – m }{(31.73)^{2}}=0.0304 \times 10^{3}\)

 

From Equation 16.52:

 

\(\theta_{\text {resonance }}=\frac{m_{1} e z^{\prime}}{I_{0}} \frac{1}{2 D_{\theta} \sqrt{1-D_{\theta}^{2}}}\) (16.52)

 

\(\begin{aligned}\theta_{\text {resonance }} &=\frac{m_{1} e z^{\prime}}{I_{0}} \frac{1}{2 D_{\theta} \sqrt{1-D_{\theta}^{2}}} \\&=\left(\frac{0.0304 \times 10^{3}}{36.768 \times 10^{5}}\right)\left[\frac{1}{(2)(0.099) \sqrt{1-(0.099)^{2}}}\right]=4.2 \times 10^{-5} rad\end{aligned}\)
