\(M_{ BC }=\frac{-4 \times 5 \times 10^{2}}{15^{2}}=-8.89 kNm , M_{ CB }=\frac{4 \times 5^{2} \times 10}{15^{2}}=4.44 kNm\)

From Eqs (16.28),

\(M_{ AB }=-\left(\frac{2 E I}{10}\right)\left(\theta_{ B }-\frac{3 v}{10}\right)=-0.2 E I \theta_{ B }+0.06 E I v\)

 

\(M_{ BA }=-\left(\frac{2 E I}{10}\right)\left(2 \theta_{ B }-\frac{3 v}{10}\right)=-0.4 E I \theta_{ B }+0.06 E I v\)

 

\(M_{ BC }=-\left(\frac{4 E I}{15}\right)\left(2 \theta_{ B }+\theta_{ C }\right)-8.89=-0.53 E I \theta_{ B }-0.27 E I \theta_{ C }-8.89\)

\(M_{ BA }+M_{ BC }=0, M_{ CB }+M_{ CD }=0\)

\(-15.5 E I \theta_{ B }-4.5 E I \theta_{ C }+E I v-148.17=0\)

 

\(-4.5 E I \theta_{ B }-15.5 E I \theta_{ C }+E I v+74.0=0\)

Since there are three unknowns a further equation is required. This is obtained by considering the shear forces at A and D. From Eq. (16.31)

\(S_{ BA }=-\frac{6 E I}{L^{2}}\left[\theta_{ A }+\theta_{ B }+\frac{2}{L}\left(v_{ A }-v_{ B }\right)\right]+S_{ BA }^{ F }\) (16.31)

\(S_{ AB }=-\left(\frac{6 E I}{10^{2}}\right) \theta_{ B }+\left(\frac{12 E I}{10^{3}}\right) v\)

 

\(S_{ DC }=-\left(\frac{6 E I}{10^{2}}\right) \theta_{ C }+\left(\frac{12 E I}{10^{3}}\right) v\)

From equilibrium

\(S_{ AB }+S_{ DC }+2=0\)

Substituting gives

\(-2.5 E I \theta_{ B }-2.5 E I \theta_{ C }+E I v+83.33=0\)

Solving gives

\(E I \theta_{ B }=-18.13, \quad E I \theta_{ C }=+2.07, \quad \text { EIv }=-123.5\)

Now substituting back in the expressions for bending moment

\(M_{ AB }=-3.78 kNm , M_{ BA }=-M_{ BC }=-0.16 kNm , M_{ CB }=-M_{ CD }=-8.24 kNm\),

\(M_{ DC }=-7.82 kNm\).
