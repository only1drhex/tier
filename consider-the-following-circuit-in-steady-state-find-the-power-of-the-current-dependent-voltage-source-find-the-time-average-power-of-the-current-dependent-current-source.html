Guest Question.

Consider the following circuit in steady state.

  • Find the power of the current-dependent voltage source.
  • Find the time-average power of the current-dependent current source.
Admin
Solution
  • \(\underline{i}_{y}=\underline{i}_{a}-\underline{i}_{b}\)
  • \(\underline{i}_{b}-\underline{i}_{c}=\underline{i}_{x}=\underline{i}_{a}\)
  • \(\operatorname{KVL}(a): 2(1+j)+(2-j 2) \underline{i}_{a}+(4+j 4)\left(\underline{i}_{a}-\underline{i}_{b}\right)=0\)
\(\longrightarrow(3+j) \underline{i}_{a}-(2+j 2) \underline{i}_{b}=-1-j\)
  • \(\operatorname{KVL}(b \& c):(4+j 4)\left(\underline{i}_{b}-\underline{i}_{a}\right)+2\left(\underline{i}_{a}-\underline{i}_{b}\right)+(4-j 2) \underline{i}_{c}=0\)
\(\longrightarrow(-2-j 4) \underline{i}_{a}+(2+j 4) \underline{i}_{b}+(4-j 2) \underline{i}_{c}=0\)
  • Then \(\underline{i}_{a}=\underline{i}_{b}=-j \mathrm{~A}\) and \(\underline{i}_{c}=0\)
  • And \(s_{2 i_{y}}=2 \underline{i}_{y} \times \underline{i}_{b}^{*}=0\)
  • \(\mathrm{KVL}(b):(4+j 4)\left(\underline{i}_{b}-\underline{i}_{a}\right)+2\left(\underline{i}_{a}-\underline{i}_{b}\right)+2\left(\underline{i}_{b}-\underline{i}_{c}\right)+\underline{v}_{x}=0 \longrightarrow \underline{v}_{x}=j 2 \mathrm{~V}\)
  • And \(s_{i_{x}}=\underline{v}_{x} \times \underline{i}_{x}^{*}=j 2 \times j=-2 \mathrm{VA}\)

And \(p_{\text {avg }, i_{x}}=-2 \mathrm{~W}\)
