Guest Question.

Obtain the following Boolean expression in a canonical SOP form.

(a) Y(A, B) = A + B (b) Y(A, B, C) = A + BC
(c) Y = AB + ACD

Admin
Solution

(a) Y(A, B) = A+B
= A ⋅ 1+B ⋅ 1
(Both variables are required in the product)
= A ⋅ \((B+\overline{B})+B ⋅(A+\overline{A})\) As \( [A+ \overline{A} = 1; B+\overline{B} = 1] \)
= AB+ \(A\overline{B}+AB+\overline{A}B\)
= AB+ \(A\overline{B}+\overline{A}B\)
= \(m_{3} + m_{2}+ m_{1}\) . . .Canonical SOP form
∴ Y(A, B) = Σ(1, 2, 3)

(b) Y(A, B, C) = A+ BC
= A ⋅ 1 ⋅ 1+BC ⋅ 1
(each term should have all three variable product)
= \(A(B+\overline{B})(C +\overline{C})+BC(A+\overline{A})\)
= A(BC + \(B\overline{C} +\overline{B}C +\overline{BC})+ABC + \overline{A}BC \)
= ABC + \(AB\overline{C} +A\overline{B}C +A\overline{BC} +\overline{A}BC\) → std. form
∴ Y(A, B, C) = (\(m_{7} +m_{6} +m_{5} +m_{4} +m_{3}\)) = Σ(3, 4, 5, 6, 7) . . .Canonical form

(c) Y(A, B, C,D) = AB+ACD
Y = AB ⋅ 1 ⋅ 1+ACD ⋅ 1 (All four variable required)
Y = \(AB(C +\overline{C})(D+\overline{D})+ACD(B+\overline{B})\)
Y = AB \( (CD+C\overline{D}+ \overline{C}D+\overline{CD}\) )+ACDB+ACD \(\overline{B}\)
Y = ABCD+ \(ABC\overline{D}+AB\overline{C}D+AB\overline{CD}+ABCD+A \overline{B}CD \)
Y = ABCD+ \(ABC\overline{D}+AB\overline{C}D+AB\overline{CD}+ABCD+ A\overline{B}CD\)
Std: form (Total possible terms =\( 2^{n} = 2^{4}\) = 16)
Y =( \(m_{15} +m_{14} +m_{13} +m_{12} +m_{11}\) )= ∑(11, 12, 13, 14, 15) → Canonical form
