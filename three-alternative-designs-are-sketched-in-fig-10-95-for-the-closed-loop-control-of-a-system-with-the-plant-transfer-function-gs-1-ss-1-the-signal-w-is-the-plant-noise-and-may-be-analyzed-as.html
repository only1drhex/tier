Guest Question.

Three alternative designs are sketched in Fig. 10.95 for the closed-loop control of a system with the plant transfer function G(s) = 1/s(s + 1). The signal w is the plant noise and may be analyzed as if it were a step; the signal v is the sensor noise and may be analyzed as if it contained power to very high frequencies.

1. (a) Compute values for the parameters \(K_{1} , a , K_{2} , K_{T} , K_{3} , d\), and \(K_{D}\) so that in each case (assuming w = 0 and v = 0),

\(\frac{Y}{R}=\frac{16}{s^{2}+4 s+16}\).

Note that in system III, a pole is to be placed at s = – 4.

(b) Complete the following table, expressing the last entries as \(A / s^{k}\) to show how fast noise from v is attenuated at high frequencies:

 

System \(K_{v}\) \(\left.\frac{y}{w}\right|_{s=0}\) \( \left.\frac{y}{v}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}\)
I

II

III

Solution

(a)

\(I: \quad \frac{Y}{R}=\frac{K_{1}}{s^{2}+a s+K_{1}}\),

\(\Longrightarrow K_{1}=16, a=4\).

\(I I \quad: \quad \frac{Y}{R}=\frac{K_{2}}{s^{2}+\left(1+K_{T}\right) s+K_{2}}\),

\(\Longrightarrow \quad K_{2}=16, \quad K_{T}=3\).

\(I I I \quad: \quad \frac{Y}{R}=\frac{K_{3}(s+4)}{s^{3}+(1+d) s^{2}+\left(K_{D}+d+K_{3}\right) s+4 K_{3}}\),

\(\frac{Y}{R}=\frac{K_{3}(s+4)}{(s+4)\left(s^{2}+\frac{d+K_{D}}{4} s+K_{3}\right)}, \text { and } K_{D}=3(d-4)\)

 

\(\Longrightarrow K_{3}=16, d=7, K_{D}=9\).

(b) \(K_{v}\):

\(E(s)=R-Y=R-\frac{16}{s^{2}+4 s+16} R=\frac{s^{2}+4 s}{s^{2}+4 s+16} R, R(s)=\frac{1}{s^{2}}\),

\(e_{\infty}=\underset{t \rightarrow \infty}{\lim} e(t)=\underset{s \rightarrow 0}{\lim} s E(s)=\frac{1}{4}\),

\(K_{v}=\frac{1}{e_{\infty}} \Longrightarrow K_{v}=4\) for all the designs.
\(\frac{Y}{W} \mid_{s=0}\) :

\(I:\left.\frac{Y}{W}\right|_{s=0}=\frac{a}{K_{1}}=\frac{1}{4}\).

\(I I:\left.\frac{Y}{W}\right|_{s=0}=\frac{1}{K_{2}}=\frac{1}{16}\).

\(III:\left.\frac{Y}{W}\right|_{s=0}=\frac{d}{4 K_{3}}=\frac{7}{64}\).

\(\left.\frac{Y}{V}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}\) :

\(I:\left.\frac{Y}{V}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}=\left.\frac{K_{1}}{s(s+a)+K_{1}}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty} \simeq \frac{K_{1}}{s^{2}}=\frac{16}{s^{2}}\).

\(I I:\left.\quad \frac{Y}{V}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}=\left.\frac{K_{2}+K_{T} s}{s^{2}+\left(1+K_{T}\right) s+K_{2}}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty} \simeq \frac{K_{T}}{s}=\frac{3}{s}\).

\(I I I:\left.\frac{Y}{V}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}=\left.\frac{K_{3}(s+4)+K_{D} s}{s(s+d)(s+1)+K_{3}(s+4)+K_{D} s}\right|_{s \rightarrow \infty}=\frac{K_{3}+K_{D}}{s^{2}}=\frac{25}{s^{2}}\).

Filling the table, and ranking the three designs:

System \(K_{v}\) \(\left.\frac{Y}{W}\right|_{s=0}\) \(\left.\frac{Y}{V}\right|_{s\rightarrow \infty}\) tracking Plant noise rejection Sensor noise rejection
I 4 1/4 16/s² Same 3 1
II 4 1/16 3/s Same 1 3
III 4 7/65 25/s² Same 2 2
