Admin
Solution

Equation [15.2]

\(C_2=C_1\left(\frac{Q_2}{Q_1} \right)^x \) [15.2]

estimates the cost of the larger system in 2000, but it must be updated by the cost index to today ‘s dollars. Equation [15.3]

\(C_2=C_1\left(\frac{Q_2}{Q_1} \right)^x\left(\frac{I_{t}}{I_0} \right) \) [15.3]

performs both operations at once. The estimated cost in current-value dollars is

\(C_2=1,700,000\left(\frac{2.0}{0.5} \right)^{0.14}\left(\frac{225}{131} \right) \)
\(=1,700,000(1.214)(1.718) = \$3,546,178\)
