The probability that the project will be completed in 50 weeks or less depends on all three paths being completed in that time. Because z for path 1-3-4-7-8 is greater than +3.00, it is treated as having a probability of completion in 50 weeks of 100 percent. It is less certain that the other two paths will be completed in that time. The probability that both will not exceed 50 is the product of their individual probabilities of completion. Thus , .2061(.9525)(1.00) = .1963.

b. The probability that the project will exceed 50 weeks is the complement of this number, which is 1.000 – .1963 =.8037.(Not that it is not the product of the path probabilities.)
