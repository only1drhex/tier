Guest Question.

Using the PSPICE Schematics editor, draw the circuit in Fig. 8.45, and use the PROBE utility to create plots for the magnitude and phase of \(V_\text{out}\), and I. At what frequency does maximum |I| occur? What are the phasors \(V_\text{out}\) and I at that frequency?

Admin
Solution

The Schematics diagram for the simulation is shown in Fig. 8.46, where an AC SWEEP has been set up for the frequency range 10 Hz to 10 MHz at 100 data points per decade. Plots for \(\mathbf{V}_{\text {out }}\) and I magnitudes and phases are given in Figs. 8.47a and b, respectively. From Fig. 8.47b we see that the maximum inductor current magnitude occurs at 31.42 kHz. At that frequency, the phasors of interest are \(\mathbf{I}=5.94 \underline{/ 16.06^{\circ}} \mathrm{A}\) and \(\mathbf{V}_{\text {out }}=299.5 \underline{/-68.15^{\circ}} \mathrm{V}\).
