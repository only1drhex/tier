Guest Question.

Refer to Figure 16.5. Given:

The weight of machine and foundation = 200 kN

The spring constant \(k=18 \times 10^{4} kN / m\)

The damping ratio D = 0.3

\(Q( kN )=Q_{0} \sin \omega t\) \(Q_{0}=60 kN\) \(\omega=130 rad / s\)

Determine: a. the amplitude of motion Z

b. the resonant frequency for vibration with damping and the amplitude of vibration at resonance

Admin
Solution

a. From Equation 16.3:

 

\(f=f_{n}=\frac{\omega_{n}}{2 \pi}=\frac{1}{2 \pi} \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}\) (16.3)

 

\(\omega_{n}=\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}=\sqrt{\frac{\left(18 \times 10^{4} kN / m \right)}{\left(\frac{200 kN }{9.81}\right)}}=93.96 rad / s\)

 

From Equation 16.11:

 

\(Z=\frac{\left(\frac{Q_{0}}{k}\right)}{\sqrt{\left[1-\left(\frac{\omega^{2}}{\omega_{n}^{2}}\right)\right]^{2}+4 D^{2}\left(\frac{\omega^{2}}{\omega_{n}^{2}}\right)}}\) (16.11)

 

Hence:

 

\(Z=\frac{\left(\frac{60}{18 \times 10^{4}}\right)}{\sqrt{\left[1-\left(\frac{130}{93.96}\right)\right]^{2}+(4)(0.3)^{2}\left(\frac{130}{93.96}\right)^{2}}}=0.00027 m =0.27 mm\)

 

b. From Equation 16.13:

 

\(f_{m}=f_{n} \sqrt{1-2 D^{2}}\) (16.13)

 

\(f_{n}=\frac{\omega_{n}}{2 \pi}=\frac{93.96}{(2)(\pi)}=14.95 cps\)

 

Thus:

 

\(f_{m}=(14.95) \sqrt{1-(2)(0.3)^{2}}=13.54 cps\)

 

From Equation 16.14:

 

\(Z_{\text {res }}=\frac{Q_{0}}{k} \frac{1}{\sqrt{\left[1-\left(1-2 D^{2}\right)\right]^{2}+4 D^{2}\left(1-2 D^{2}\right)}}=\frac{Q_{0}}{k} \frac{1}{2 D \sqrt{1-D^{2}}}\) (16.14)

 

\(Z_{\text {res }}=\frac{60}{18 \times 10^{4}} \times \frac{1}{2 \times 0.3 \times \sqrt{1-0.3^{2}}} m=0.58 mm\)
