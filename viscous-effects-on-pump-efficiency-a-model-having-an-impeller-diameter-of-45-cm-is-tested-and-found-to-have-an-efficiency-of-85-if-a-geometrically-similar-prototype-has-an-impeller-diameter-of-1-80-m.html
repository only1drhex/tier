VISCOUS EFFECTS ON PUMP EFFICIENCY

A model having an impeller diameter of 45 cm is tested and found to have an efficiency of 85%. If a geometrically similar prototype has an impeller diameter of 1.80 m, estimate its efficiency when it is operating under conditions that are dynamically similar to those in the model test \(\left(C_{Q, \text { model }}=C_{Q, \text { prototype }}\right)\).

Problem Definition

Situation: Model with 45 cm impeller has 85% efficiency.

Find: Efficiency of pump with 1.6 m impeller.

Assumptions: Efficiency difference due to viscous effects.

Plan

Use Eq. (14.18) to determine viscous effects.

\(\frac{1-e_{1}}{1-e}=\left(\frac{D}{D_{1}}\right)^{1 / 5}\) (14.18)

Admin
Solution

Efficiency

 

\(e=1-\frac{1-e_{1}}{\left(D / D_{1}\right)^{1 / 5}}=1-\frac{0.15}{1.32}=1-0.11=0.89\)

 

or

 

e = 89%
