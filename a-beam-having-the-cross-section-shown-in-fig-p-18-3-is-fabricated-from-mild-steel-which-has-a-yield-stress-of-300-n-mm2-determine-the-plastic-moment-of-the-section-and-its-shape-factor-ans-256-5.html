Guest Question.

A beam having the cross section shown in Fig. P.18.3 is fabricated from mild steel which has a yield stress of \(300 N / mm ^{2}\). Determine the plastic moment of the section and its shape factor.
Ans. 256.5 kNm, 1.52.

Admin
Solution

In this case the beam section does not have a horizontal axis of symmetry so that the elastic and plastic neutral axes do not coincide. In Fig. S.18.3

\((75 \times 15+270 \times 15+250 \times 15) \bar{y}=75 \times 15 \times 292.5+270 \times 15 \times 150+250 \times 15 \times 7.5\)

from which

\(\bar{y}=108.1 mm\)

Then

\(I_{z}=\frac{75 \times 15^{3}}{12}+75 \times 15 \times 184.4^{2}+\frac{15 \times 270^{3}}{12}+270 \times 15 \times 41.9^{2}\)

 

\(+\frac{250 \times 15^{3}}{12}+250 \times 15 \times 100.6^{2}\)

i.e.

\(I_{z}=108.0 \times 10^{6} mm ^{4}\)

Then

\(Z_{ e }=\frac{108.0 \times 10^{6}}{191.9}=562847.8 mm ^{3}\)

Also, since the areas above and below the plastic neutral axis are equal

\(250 \times 15+15 \bar{y}_{ P }=75 \times 15+15\left(270-\bar{y}_{ P }\right)\)

which gives

\(\bar{y}_{ P }=47.5 mm\)

Then

\(Z_{ P }=75 \times 15 \times 230.0+15 \times 222.5 \times 111.25+250 \times 15 \times 55.5+15 \times 47.5 \times 23.75\)

i.e.

\(Z_{ P }=855093.8 mm ^{3}\)

Then

\(f=\frac{855093.8}{562847.8}=1.52\)

and

\(M_{ P }=855093.8 \times 300 \times 10^{-6}=256.5 kNm\).
