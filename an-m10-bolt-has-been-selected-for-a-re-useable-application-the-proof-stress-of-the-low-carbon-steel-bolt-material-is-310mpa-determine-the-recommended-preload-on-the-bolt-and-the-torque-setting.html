Guest Question.

An M10 bolt has been selected for a re-useable application. The proof stress of the low carbon steel bolt material is 310MPa. Determine the recommended preload on the bolt and the torque setting.

Admin
Solution

From Table 12.2, the pitch for a coarse series M10 bolt is 1.5mm.

\( d_{P} = 10 – 0.649519 \times 1.5 = 9.026 mm \)
\( d_{r} = 10 – 1.226869 \times 1.5 = 8.160 mm \)
\( A_{t} = \frac{\pi}{16} (9.026 + 8.16)^{2} = 57.99 mm^{2} \)

For a reusable connection, the recommended preload is

\( F_{i} = 0.75 A_{t} \sigma _{P} = 13.48 kN \)

From Table 12.5,K = 0.2.The torque required to tighten the bolt is given by

\( T = KF_{i}d = 0.2 \times 13.48 \times 10^{3} \times 0.01 \)
\( = 26.96 Nm \)
