Guest Question.

Alpha Taxi and Hauling also plans to have cabs at a new rail station. The expected arrival rate is 4.8 customers per hour, and the service rate (including return time to the rail station) is exected to be . How many cabs will be needed to achieve an average time in line of 20 minutes or less?

Admin
Solution

λ= 4.8 customers per hour

µ =1.5 customers per hour

M = ?

\(r = \frac{\lambda }{\mu } = \frac{4.8}{1.5} = 3.2\) customers

\(W_q\) (desired)=20 minutes, or .333 hour

Using \(L_q = λ × W_q\) , you can solve for \(L_q\) : 4.8/hour (.333 hour) 1.6 units. Thus, the average number waiting should not exceed 1.6 customers. Refer Table 18.4
