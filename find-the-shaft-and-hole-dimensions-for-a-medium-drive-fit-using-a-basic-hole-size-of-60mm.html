Guest Question.

Find the shaft and hole dimensions for a medium drive fit using a basic hole size of 60mm.

Admin
Solution

From Table 15.1 the ISO symbol is 60H7/s6.

From data sheet BS4500A (Table 15.2) the hole tolerance is \( \begin{matrix} +0.030 \\ 0.000 \end{matrix} mm \) and the shaft tolerance is \( \begin{matrix} +0.072 \\ +0.053\end{matrix} mm \).

So the hole should be given dimensions 60.00 \( \begin{matrix} +0.030 \\ 0.000 \end{matrix} mm \) and the shaft 60.00 \( \begin{matrix} +0.072 \\ +0.053\end{matrix} mm \).
