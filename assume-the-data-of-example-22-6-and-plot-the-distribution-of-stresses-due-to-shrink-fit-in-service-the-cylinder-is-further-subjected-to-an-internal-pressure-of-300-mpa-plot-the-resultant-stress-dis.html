Guest Question.

Assume the data of and plot the distribution of stresses due to shrink fit. In service, the cylinder is further subjected to an internal pressure of 300 MPa. Plot the resultant stress distribution.

Admin
Solution

\( \text { Given } \quad D_{1}=20 mm \quad D_{2}=40 mm \quad D_{3}=60 mm \).

\( \sigma_{\max .}=100 N / mm ^{2} \quad E=207 kN / mm ^{2} \).

\( P_{i}=300 MPa \).

Step I Stresses due to shrink fit
(a) Jacket The jacket is subjected to an internal pressure of 38.46 N/mm² due to shrink fit. From Eqs (22.6) and (22.7),

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P_{i} D_{i}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{o}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \) (22.6).

\( \sigma_{t}=+\frac{P_{i} D_{i}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{o}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}+1\right] \) (22.7).

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P D_{2}^{2}}{\left(D_{3}^{2}-D_{2}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{3}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \).

\( =-\frac{(38.46)(40)^{2}}{\left(60^{2}-40^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{60^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \).

\( =-30.77\left[\left(\frac{30}{r}\right)^{2}-1\right] \).

and \( \sigma_{t}=+\frac{P D_{2}^{2}}{\left(D_{3}^{2}-D_{2}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{3}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}+1\right] \).

\( =+30.77\left[\left(\frac{30}{r}\right)^{2}+1\right] \).

The stresses in the jacket are tabulated as follows:

30 28 26 24 22 20 r
0 -5 -10 -17 -26 -38 \(\sigma_{r}\)
62 66 72 79 88 100 \(\sigma_{t}\)

(b) Inner tube The inner tube is subjected to an external pressure of 38.46 N/mm² due to shrink fit.
From Eqs (22.16) and (22.17),

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P_{o} D_{o}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[1-\frac{D_{i}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}\right] \) (22.16).

\( \sigma_{t}=-\frac{P_{o} D_{o}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[1+\frac{D_{i}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}\right] \) (22.17).

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P D_{2}^{2}}{\left(D_{2}^{2}-D_{1}^{2}\right)}\left[1-\frac{D_{1}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}\right] \).

\( =-\frac{(38.46)(40)^{2}}{\left(40^{2}-20^{2}\right)}\left[1-\frac{20^{2}}{4 r^{2}}\right] \).

\( =-51.28\left[1-\left(\frac{10}{r}\right)^{2}\right] \).

and \( \sigma_{t}=-\frac{P D_{2}^{2}}{\left(D_{2}^{2}-D_{1}^{2}\right)}\left[1+\frac{D_{1}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}\right] \).

\( =-51.28\left[1+\left(\frac{10}{r}\right)^{2}\right] \).

The stresses in the tube are tabulated as follows:

20 18 16 14 12 10 r
-38 -35 -31 -25 -16 0 \(\sigma_{r}\)
-64 -67 -71 -77 -87 -103 \(\sigma_{t}\)

Step II Stresses due to internal pressure
When the compound cylinder is subjected to an internal pressure of 300 MPa in service, the stresses are calculated from Eqs (22.6) and (22.7).

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P_{i} D_{i}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{o}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \) (22.6).

\( \sigma_{t}=+\frac{P_{i} D_{i}^{2}}{\left(D_{o}^{2}-D_{i}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{o}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}+1\right] \) (22.7).

\( \sigma_{r}=-\frac{P_{i} D_{1}^{2}}{\left(D_{3}^{2}-D_{1}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{3}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \).

\( =-\frac{(300)(20)^{2}}{\left(60^{2}-20^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{60^{2}}{4 r^{2}}-1\right] \).

\( =-37.5\left[\left(\frac{30}{r}\right)^{2}-1\right] \).

and \( \sigma_{t}=+\frac{P_{i} D_{1}^{2}}{\left(D_{3}^{2}-D_{1}^{2}\right)}\left[\frac{D_{3}^{2}}{4 r^{2}}+1\right] \).

\( =+37.5\left[\left(\frac{30}{r}\right)^{2}+1\right] \).

The stresses in the compound cylinder are tabulated as follows:

30 28 26 24 22 20 18 16 14 12 10 r
0 -6 -12 -21 -32 -47 -67 -94 -135 -197 -300 \(\sigma_{r}\)
75 81 87 96 107 122 142 169 210 272 375 \(\sigma_{t}\)

The resultant stresses in the compound cylinder are obtained by superimposition of stresses due to shrink fi t and those due to internal pressure. They are tabulated as follows:
