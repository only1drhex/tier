Transforming the concrete section into an equivalent steel section,

\(E_{c}=w_{c} ^{1.5}\sqrt{f^{′} _{c} }\)

 

\(=(145)^{1.5} \sqrt{3.5}= 3266 ksi\)

 

\(n=\frac{E_{s} }{E_{c} }\)

 

\(n=\frac{29,000 }{3266} =8.87\)

 

\(A_{ct}=\frac{x_{t} }{n}\)

 

\(=\frac{(80)(2)}{8.87}=18.02in^{2}\)

Using the top of the concrete slab as the datum, we develop Table 7-2.
