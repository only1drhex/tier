Guest Question.

Derive the stiffness matrix of a constant strain, triangular finite element 123 of thickness t and coordinates (0, 0), (2, 0) and (0, 3), respectively, to be used for plane stress problems. The elements of the elasticity matrix [D] are as follows.
\(D_{11}=D_{22}=a, \quad D_{12}=b, \quad D_{13}=D_{23}=0, \quad D_{33}=c\)
where a, b and c are material constants.
Ans. See Solutions Manual.

Admin
Solution

Assume displacement functions

\(w=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2} x+\alpha_{3} y\)

 

\(v=\alpha_{4}+\alpha_{5} x+\alpha_{6} y\)

Then

\(w_{1}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(0)+\alpha_{3}(0)\)

 

\(w_{2}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(2)+\alpha_{3}(0)\)

 

\(w_{3}=\alpha_{1}+\alpha_{2}(0)+\alpha_{3}(3)\)

Solving

\(\alpha_{1}=w_{1}\)

 

\(\alpha_{2}=\frac{w_{2}-w_{1}}{2}\)

 

\(\alpha_{3}=\frac{w_{3}-w_{1}}{3}\)

Substituting in the displacement functions

\(w=\left[1-\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)-\left(\frac{y}{3}\right)\right] w_{1}+\left(\frac{x}{2}\right) w_{2}+\left(\frac{y}{3}\right) w_{3}\)

so that

\(\frac{\partial w}{\partial x}=-\left(\frac{w_{1}}{2}\right)+\left(\frac{w_{2}}{2}\right)\)

 

\(\frac{\partial w}{\partial y}=-\left(\frac{w_{1}}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{w_{3}}{3}\right)\)

Similarly

\(\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=-\left(\frac{v_{1}}{2}\right)+\left(\frac{v_{2}}{2}\right)\)

 

\(\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}=-\left(\frac{v_{1}}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{v_{3}}{3}\right)\)

Then the [B] matrix is given by

\([ B ]=\left[\begin{array}{c}\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} \\\frac{\partial v}{\partial y} \\\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\end{array}\right]=\frac{1}{6}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}-3 & 0 & 3 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\0 & -2 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 2 \\-2 & -3 & 0 & 3 & 2 & 0\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}w_{1} \\v_{1} \\w_{2} \\v_{2} \\w_{3} \\v_{3}\end{array}\right]\)

Also the [D] matrix is

\([ D ]=\left[\begin{array}{lll}a & b & 0 \\b & a & 0 \\0 & 0 & c\end{array}\right]\)

so that

\([ D ][ B ]=\frac{1}{6}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}-3 a & -2 b & 3 a & 0 & 0 & 2 b \\-3 b & -2 a & 3 b & 0 & 0 & 2 a \\-2 c & -3 c & 0 & 3 c & 2 c & 0\end{array}\right]\)

and

\([ B ]^{ T }[ D ][ B ]=\frac{1}{36}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}9 a+4 c & 6 b+6 c & -9 a & -6 c & -4 c & -6 b \\6 b+6 c & 4 a+9 c & -6 b & -9 c & -6 c & -4 a \\-9 a & -6 b & 9 a & 0 & 0 & 6 b \\-6 c & -9 c & 0 & 9 c & 6 c & 0 \\-4 c & -6 c & 0 & 6 c & 4 c & 0 \\-6 b & -4 a & 6 b & 0 & 0 & 4 a\end{array}\right]\)

The stiffness matrix for the element is

\([ K ]=\int_{\text {area }}[ B ]^{ T }[ D ][ B ] t d x d y\)

from which

\([ K ]=3 t[ B ]^{ T }[ D ][ B ]\).
