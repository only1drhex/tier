Guest Question.

The frame shown in Fig. P.16.36 has rigid joints at B, C and D and is pinned to its foundation at A and G. The joint Dis prevented from moving horizontally by the member DF which is pinned to a support at F. The flexural rigidity of the members AB and BC is 2EI while that of all other members is EI.
Use the moment distribution method to calculate the end moments in the members.

Ans. \(M_{ BA }=-M_{ BC }=2.6 kNm , M_{ CB }=-M_{ CD }=67.7 kNm , M_{ DC }=-53.5 kNm\),

\(M_{ DF }=26.7 kNm , M_{ DG }=26.7 kNm\).

Admin
Solution

The distribution factors are

\(DF _{ BA }=\frac{\left(\frac{3 \times 2 E I}{6}\right)}{\left[\left(\frac{3 \times 2 E I}{6}\right)+\left(\frac{4 \times 2 E I}{6}\right)\right]}=\frac{3}{7}, DF _{ BC }=\frac{4}{7}\)

 

\(DF _{ CB }=\frac{\left(\frac{4 \times 2 E I}{6}\right)}{\left[\left(\frac{4 \times 2 E I}{6}\right)+\left(\frac{4 E I}{3}\right)\right]}=\frac{1}{2}, DF _{ CD }=\frac{1}{2}\)

 

\(DF _{ DC }=\frac{\left(\frac{4 E I}{3}\right)}{\left[\left(\frac{4 E I}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{3 E I}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{3 E I}{3}\right)\right]}=\frac{4}{10}\)

Similarly

\(DF _{ DF }=\frac{3}{10}\) and \(DF _{ DG }=\frac{3}{10}\)

The fixed end moments for the no-sway case are

\(M_{ BC }^{ F }=-M_{ CB }^{ F }=-\frac{14 \times 6^{2}}{12}=-42 kNm\)

The no-sway moment distribution is carried out in Table S.16.36(a).

Suppose that the member AB is given an arbitrary sway Î¸ as shown in Fig. S.16.36.
The arbitrary sway FEMs are then

\(AB : \frac{3 \times 2 E I \times 6 \theta}{6^{2}} ; CD , DC : \frac{6 \times E I \times 6 \theta}{3^{2}}\)

The ratio of these arbitrary sway moments is 1 : 4, therefore assume values of 70 and 280 kNm.
The arbitrary sway moment distribution is carried out in Table S.16.36(b).

Referring to Fig. S.16.36 the sway equilibrium equation is

\(M_{ BA }(\theta)+M_{ CD }(2 \theta)+M_{ DC }(2 \theta)+40 \times 6 \theta=0\)

i.e.

\(M_{ BA }+2\left(M_{ CD }+M_{ DC }\right)+240=0\)

Then

24.5 + 2(-27.6-9.1) + k[65.5 + 2(119.8 + 132.4)]+ 240 = 0

which gives

k = -0.335

The final end moments are then

\(M_{ BA }=-M_{ BC }=24.5-0.335 \times 65.5=+2.6 kNm\)

Similarly

\(M_{ CB }=-M_{ CD }=67.7 kNm , \quad M_{ DC }=-53.5 kNm , \quad M_{ DF }=26.7 kNm\),

\(M_{ DG }=26.7 kNm\) .
