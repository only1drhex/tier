Guest Question.

A digital filter is characterized by the state-space equations in Eqs. (8.4a) and (8.4b) with

\(q (n T+T)= A q (n T)+ b x(n T)\) (8.4a)

\(y(n T)= c ^{T} q (n T)+d x(n T)\) (8.4b)

\(A =\left[\begin{array}{ccc}-\frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{3} & -\frac{1}{4} \\1 & 0 & 0 \\0 & 1 & 0\end{array}\right] \quad b =\left[\begin{array}{l}2 \\0 \\0\end{array}\right] \quad c ^{T}=\left[\begin{array}{lll}-\frac{1}{4} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{1}{12}\end{array}\right] \quad d=2\)

Obtain a direct canonic realization.

Admin
Solution

In order to obtain a direct canonic realization, we need to deduce the transfer function of the filter. From Eq. (5.9) and , we have

 

\(\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}=H(z)=\frac{N(z)}{D(z)}= c ^{T}(z I – A )^{-1} b +d\) (5.9)

 

\(\frac{Y(z)}{X(z)}=H(z)=\frac{N(z)}{D(z)}= c ^{T}(z I – A )^{-1} b +d\)

 

\(=\frac{1}{\operatorname{det}(z I – A )}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}-\frac{1}{4} &\frac{1}{6} & \frac{1}{12}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}z^{2} & \frac{1}{3} z+\frac{1}{4} & -\frac{1}{4} z \\-z & \left(z+\frac{1}{2}\right) z & \frac{1}{4} \\1 & -\left(z+\frac{1}{2}\right) & \left(z+\frac{1}{2}\right) z+\frac{1}{3}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l}2 \\0 \\0\end{array}\right]+2\)

 

\(=\frac{1}{\operatorname{det}(z I – A )}\left[\begin{array}{ccc}-\frac{1}{4} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{1}{12}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}2 z^{2} \\-2 z \\2\end{array}\right]+2\)

 

where

 

\(\operatorname{det}(z I – A )=z^{3}+\frac{1}{2} z^{2}+\frac{1}{3} z+\frac{1}{4}\)

 

Thus polynomials N(z) and D(z) can be deduced as

 

\(N(z)=\left[\begin{array}{lll}-\frac{1}{4} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{1}{12}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}2 z^{2} \\-2 z \\2\end{array}\right]+2 \operatorname{det}(z I – A )\)

 

\(N(z)=\left[\begin{array}{lll}-\frac{1}{4} & \frac{1}{6} & \frac{1}{12}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}2 z^{2} \\-2 z \\2\end{array}\right]+2 \operatorname{det}(z I – A )\)

 

\(=2 z^{3}+\frac{1}{2} z^{2}+\frac{1}{3} z+\frac{2}{3}\)

 

and

 

\(D(z)=\operatorname{det}(z I – A )=z^{3}+\frac{1}{2} z^{2}+\frac{1}{3} z+\frac{1}{4}\)

 

respectively. Therefore,

 

\(H(z)=\frac{2 z^{3}+\frac{1}{2} z^{2}+\frac{1}{3} z+\frac{2}{3}}{z^{3}+\frac{1}{2} z^{2}+\frac{1}{3} z+\frac{1}{4}}=\frac{2+\frac{1}{2} z^{-1}+\frac{1}{3} z^{-2}+\frac{2}{3} z^{-3}}{1+\frac{1}{2} z^{-1}+\frac{1}{3} z^{-2}+\frac{1}{4} z^{-3}}\)

 

The required realization is shown in Fig. 8.8 where

 

\(a_{0}=2 \quad a_{1}=\frac{1}{2} \quad a_{2}=\frac{1}{3} \quad a_{3}=\frac{2}{3}\)

 

\(b_{1}=\frac{1}{2} \quad b_{2}=\frac{1}{3} \quad b_{3}=\frac{1}{4}\)
