Guest Question.

Consider the control of

\(G(s)=\frac{Y(s)}{U(s)}=\frac{10}{s(s+1)}\).

a) Let \(y = x_{1} and \dot{x_{1}} = x_{2}\), and write state equations for the system.

b) Find \(K_{1} and K_{2}\) so that \(u = - K_{1}x_{1} - K_{2}x_{2}\) yields closed-loop poles with a natural frequency \(\omega_{n} = 3\) and a damping ratio ζ = 0.5.

c) Design a state estimator for the system that yields estimator error poles with \(\omega_{n1} = 15\) and \(\zeta_{1} = 0.5\).

d) What is the transfer function of the controller obtained by combining parts (a) through (c)?

e) Sketch the root locus of the resulting closed-loop system as plant gain (nominally 10) is varied.

Admin
Solution

The state equations are,

\(\left[\begin{array}{l}\dot{x}_{1} \\ \dot{x}_{2}\end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & -1\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{l} x_{1} \\ x_{2}\end{array}\right]+\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 10\end{array}\right] u\),

\(y=\left[\begin{array}{ll} 1 & 0\end{array}\right] x\).

(b) \(K = \operatorname{place}(F,G, \operatorname{roots}([1 2 ∗ \operatorname{zeta} ∗ \text{wn} \text{wn}ˆ 2]))= [0.9 0.2]\).

(c) \(L=\operatorname{place}\left( F ^{\prime}, H ^{\prime}, \operatorname{roots}\left(\left[\begin{array}{ll} 1 2 * \operatorname{zeta} * \text { wn } \text{wn}ˆ2\end{array}\right]\right)\right)^{\prime}=\left[\begin{array}{ll} 14 & 211\end{array}\right]^{T}\).

(d) The transfer function for the controller is,

\(D_{c}(s)=- K (s I – F + G K + L H )^{-1} L\)

 

\(=\frac{-(54.8 s+202.5)}{s^{2}+17 s+262}\).
