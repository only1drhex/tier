The Substitution Steps

Complete the problem initiated in by generating the final solution with forward and back substitution.

Admin
Solution

As stated above, the intent of forward substitution is to impose the elimination manipulations, which we had previously applied to [A], on the right-hand-side vector {B}. Recall that the system being solved in was

\(\begin{bmatrix} 3 &−0.1& −0.2\\0.1 &7 &−0.3\\0.3 &−0.2& 10\end{bmatrix}\begin{Bmatrix}x_{1} \\x_{2} \\x_{3} \end{Bmatrix} =\begin{Bmatrix} 7.85\\−19.3\\71.4 \end{Bmatrix}\)

and that the forward-elimination phase of conventional Gauss elimination resulted in

\(\begin{bmatrix} 3 &−0.1 &−0.2\\ 0 &7.00333& −0.293333\\0& 0& 10.0120 \end{bmatrix}\begin{Bmatrix} x_{1}\\x_{2}\\ x_{3} \end{Bmatrix} =\begin{Bmatrix}7.85\\−19.5617\\70.0843 \end{Bmatrix} \) (E10.2.1)

The forward-substitution phase is implemented by applying Eq. (10.7) to this case,

\(\begin{bmatrix} 1 &0 &0\\0.0333333 &1 &0\\ 0.100000& −0.0271300 &1\end{bmatrix} \begin{Bmatrix} d_{1}\\d_{2}\\d_{3} \end{Bmatrix} =\begin{Bmatrix}7.85\\ −19.3\\71.4 \end{Bmatrix} \)

 

\([L][U] = [A] \) (10.7)

or, multiplying out the left-hand side,

\( \begin{matrix} d_{1}&&=7.85\\ 0.0333333d_1 +& d_{2}&= −19.3 \\ &0.1d_1 − 0.02713d_2 + d_3 = 71.4\end{matrix} \)

We can solve the first equation for \( d_1\),

\(d_1 = 7.85\)

which can be substituted into the second equation to for

\(d_2 = −19.3 − 0.0333333(7.85) = −19.5617\)

Both \(d_1\) and d2 can be substituted into the third equation to give

\(d_3 = 71.4 − 0.1(7.85) + 0.02713(−19.5617) = 70.0843\)

Thus,

\(\left\{D\right\}=\begin{Bmatrix} 7.85\\−19.5617\\ 70.0843\end{Bmatrix} \)

which is identical to the right-hand side of Eq. (E10.2.1).
This result can then be substituted into Eq. (10.4), [U]{X} = {D}, to give

\(\begin{bmatrix} 3 &−0.1& −0.2 \\0 &7.00333 &−0.293333\\ 0& 0& 10.0120 \end{bmatrix} \begin{Bmatrix} x_{1}\\x_{2}\\ x_{3} \end{Bmatrix} =\begin{Bmatrix} 7.85\\−19.5617\\70.0843\end{Bmatrix} \)

 

[U]{X} − {D} = 0 (10.4)

which can be solved by back substitution (see for details) to obtain the final solution,

\(\left\{X\right\}=\begin{Bmatrix} 3\\-2.5\\ 7.00003\end{Bmatrix} \)
