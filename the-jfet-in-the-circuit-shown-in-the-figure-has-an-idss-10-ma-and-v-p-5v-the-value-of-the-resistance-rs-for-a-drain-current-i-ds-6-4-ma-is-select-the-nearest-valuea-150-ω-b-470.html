Guest Question.

The JFET in the circuit shown in the figure has an \(I_{ DSS }\) = 10 mA and \(V_{P}=-5 V\). The value of the resistance \(R_{S}\) for a drain current \(I_{D S}=6.4\) mA is (Select the Nearest value)

(a) 150 Ω (b) 470 Ω

(c) 560 Ω (d) 1 kΩ

Admin
Solution

Using the formula for drain current,

\(\begin{gathered}I_{D S}=I_{D S S}\left(1-\frac{V_{G S}}{V p}\right)^{2} \\6.4 \times 10^{-3}=10 \times 10^{-3}\left(1-\frac{V_{G S}}{-5}\right)^{2} \\0.64=\left(1+\frac{V_{G S}}{5}\right)^{2} \\\Rightarrow 1+\frac{V_{G S}}{5}=0.8 \\\Rightarrow \frac{V_{G S}}{5}=-0.2\end{gathered}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\Rightarrow V_{G S}=-1 V \\&\Rightarrow V_{G S}=-I_{D S} R_{S} \\&-1=-6.4 \times 10^{-3} R_{S} \\&R_{S}=\frac{1}{6.4 \times 10^{-3}}=156 \Omega \\&R_{S} \cong 150 \Omega\end{aligned}\)
