Guest Question.

Simplify the following:

(a) f(t)=\(5 \cos(2 t+15^{\circ})-4 \sin (2 t-30^{\circ}).\)

(b) g(t)=\(8 \sin t+4 \cos \left(t+50^{\circ}\right)\)

(c) h(t)=\(\int_{0}^{t}(10 \cos 40 t+50 \sin 40 t) d t\)

Admin
Solution

(a) \(F=5 \angle 15^{\circ}-4 \angle-30^{\circ}-90^{\circ}=6.8296+j 4.758=8.3236 \angle 34.86^{\circ}\)

\(f(t)=8.324 \cos \left(30 t+34.86^{\circ}\right)\)

(b) \(G=8 \angle-90^{\circ}+4 \angle 50^{\circ}=2.571-j 4.9358=5.565 \angle-62.49^{\circ}\)

\(g(t)=5.565 \cos \left(t-62.49^{\circ}\right)\)

(c) \(\mathrm{H}=\frac{1}{\mathrm{j} \omega}\left(10 \angle 0^{\circ}+50 \angle-90^{\circ}\right), \quad \omega=40\)

i.e. \(\mathrm{H}=0.25 \angle-90^{\circ}+1.25 \angle-180^{\circ}=-\mathrm{j} 0.25-1.25=1.2748 \angle-168.69^{\circ}\)

\(\mathrm{h}(\mathrm{t})=1.2748 \cos \left(40 \mathrm{t}-168.69^{\circ}\right)\)
