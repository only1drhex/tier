Guest Question.

A gas turbine unit receives air at 1 bar and 300 K and compresses it adiabatically to 6.2 bar. The compressor efficiency is 88%. The fuel has a heating value of 44186 kJ/kg and the fuel air ratio is 0.017 kJ/kg of air. The turbine internal efficiency is 90%. Calculate the work of turbine and compressor per kg of air compressed and thermal efficiency.For products of combustion, \(c_{p}\)= 1.147 kJ/kg K and γ = 1.333.

Admin
Solution
\(\text { Given }: p_{1}\left(=p_{4}\right)=1 \text { bar, } T_{1}=300 K ; p_{2}\left(=p_{3}\right)=6.2 \text { bar } ; \eta_{\text {compressor }}=88 \% \text { ; }\)

 

C = 44186 kJ/kg ; fuel-air ratio = 0.017 kJ/kg of air,

 

\(\eta_{\text {turbine }}=90 \%, C_{p}=1.147 kJ / kg K ; \gamma=1.333\)

 

For isentropic compression process 1-2 :

 

\(\frac{T_{2}}{T_{1}}=\left(\frac{p_{2}}{p_{1}}\right)^{\frac{\gamma-1}{\gamma}}=\left(\frac{6.2}{1}\right)^{1.4}=1.684\)

 

\(\therefore \quad T_{2}=300 \times 1.684=505.2 K\)

 

\(\text { Now, } \quad \eta_{\text {compressor }}=\frac{T_{2}-T_{1}}{T_{2}^{\prime}-T_{1}}\)

 

\(0.88=\frac{505.2-300}{T_{2}^{\prime}-300}\)

 

\(T_{2}^{\prime}=\left(\frac{505.2-300}{0.88}+300\right)=533.2 K\)

 

Heat supplied

 

\(=\left(m_{a}+m_{f}\right) \times c_{p}\left(T_{3}-T_{2}{ }^{\prime}\right)=m_{f} \times C\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { or }\\&\left(1+\frac{m_{f}}{m_{a}}\right) \times c_{p}\left(T_{3}-T_{2}^{\prime}\right)=\frac{m_{f}}{m_{a}} \times C\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\text { or }\\&(1+0.017) \times 1.005\left(T_{3}-533.2\right)\end{aligned}\)

 

= 0.017 × 44186

 

\(\therefore\) \(T_{3}=\frac{0.017 \times 44186}{(1+0.017) \times 1.005}+533.2=1268 K\)

 

For isentropic expansion process 3-4 :

 

\(\frac{T_{4}}{T_{3}}=\left(\frac{p_{4}}{p_{3}}\right)^{\frac{\gamma-1}{\gamma}}=\left(\frac{1}{6.2}\right)^{\frac{1.333-1}{1.333}}=0.634\)

 

\(\therefore \quad T_{4}=1268 \times 0.634=803.9 K \quad\left(\because \quad \gamma_{g}=1.333 \ldots . . \text { Given }\right)\)

 

\(\text { Now, } \quad \eta_{\text {turbine }}=\frac{T_{3}-T_{4}^{\prime}}{T_{3}-T_{4}}\)

 

\(0.9=\frac{1268-T_{4}^{\prime}}{1268-803.9}\)

 

\(\therefore \quad T_{4}^{\prime}=1268-0.9(1268-803.9)=850.3 K\)

 

\(W_{\text {compressor }}=c_{p}\left(T_{2}^{\prime}-T_{1}\right)=1.005(533.2-300)=234.4 kJ / kg\)

 

\(W_{\text {turbine }}=c_{p g}\left(T_{3}-T_{4}^{\prime}\right)=1.147(1268-850.3)=479.1 kJ / kg\)

 

\(\text { Network }=W_{\text {turbine }}-W_{\text {compressor }}\)

 

= 479.1 – 234.4 = 244.7 kJ/kg

 

Heat supplied per kg of air = 0.017 × 44186 = 751.2 kJ/kg

 

\(\text { Thermal efficiency, } \quad \eta_{\text {th }}=\frac{\text { Net work }}{\text { Heat supplied }}\)

 

\(=\frac{244.7}{751.2}= 0 . 3 2 5 7 \quad \text { or } \quad 3 2 . 5 7 \%\)
