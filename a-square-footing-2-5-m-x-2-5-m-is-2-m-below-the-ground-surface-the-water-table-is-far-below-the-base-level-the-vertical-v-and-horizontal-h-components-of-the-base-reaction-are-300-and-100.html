Guest Question.

A square footing \((2.5 \mathrm{~m} \times 2.5 \mathrm{~m})\) is \(2 \mathrm{~m}\) below the ground surface. The water table is far below the base level. The vertical (V) and horizontal (H) components of the base reaction are 300 and \(100 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}\), respectively. The eccentricity of the base reaction is \(0.2 \mathrm{~m}\). The unit weight of the soil is \(17.5 \mathrm{kN} / \mathrm{m}^{3}\), and the effective shear strength parameters are \(c^{\prime}=0\) and \(\phi=32\). Determine the ultimate bearing capacity of the foundation.

Admin
Solution

From Figure \(9.2: \phi=32^{\circ}: N_{c}=35.5 ; N_{q}=23.2 ; N_{\gamma}=20.8\) (Hansen’s factor)

\(B^{\prime}=B-2 e=2.5-2 \times 0.2=2.1 \mathrm{~m}\)

From Table \(9.1: s_{q}=1.45 ; s_{\gamma}=0.66\)
