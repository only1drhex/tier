Guest Question.

Assume that Gilbert and Bufton (see ) started the year with capital account balances of £25,900 and £15,750 respectively. During the year, Gilbert drew out £35,000 from the partnership and Bufton took £17,000.
Show the relevant extract from the partnership’s statement of financial position as at 31 December 2013.
