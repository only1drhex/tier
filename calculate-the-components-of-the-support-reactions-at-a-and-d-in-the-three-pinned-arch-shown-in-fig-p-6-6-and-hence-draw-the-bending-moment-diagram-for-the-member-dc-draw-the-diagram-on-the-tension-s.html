Guest Question.

Calculate the components of the support reactions at A and D in the three-pinned arch shown in Fig. P.6.6 and hence draw the bending moment diagram for the member DC; draw the diagram on the tension side of the member. All members are 1.5 m long.

Admin
Solution

Referring to Fig. S.6.6 the calculated dimensions are as shown. Taking moments about D

\( R_{ A , V } \times 2.05-R_{ A , H } \times 3.55+5 \times 0.75+15 \times 1.5=0 \)

i.e.

\( R_{ A , V }-1.73 R_{ A , H }+12.8=0 \) (i)

Taking moments about B

\( R_{ A , V } \times 1.3-R_{ A , H } \times 0.75=0 \)

i.e.

\( R_{ A , V }-0.58 R_{ A , H }=0 \) (ii)

Then, Eq. (i)â€“Eq. (ii) gives

\( R_{ A , H }=11.13 kN \)

Then, from Eq. (ii)

\( R_{ A , V }=6.46 kN \)

Now resolving horizontally

\( R_{ D , H }=11.13-15=-3.87 kN \text { (acting to the right) }\)

and resolving vertically

\( R_{ D , V }=6.46+5+10=21.46 kN \)

The bending moment at C is given by

\( M_{ C }=3.87 \times 1.5=5.81 kNm \)

Also the bending moment at D is zero. The bending moment varies linearly in DC and is drawn on the left hand side of the member.
