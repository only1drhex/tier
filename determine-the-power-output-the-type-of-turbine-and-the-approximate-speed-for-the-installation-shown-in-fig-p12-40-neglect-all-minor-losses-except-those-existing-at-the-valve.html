Guest Question.

Determine the power output, the type of turbine, and the approximate speed for the installation shown in Fig. P12.40. Neglect all minor losses except those existing at the valve.

Admin
Solution

Write energy eqn. from upper reservoir (loc. 1) to surge tank (loc. 2) and solve for Q:

 

\(\begin{aligned}Q &=\left[2 g A^{2} \frac{D}{f L}\left(z_{1}-z_{2}\right)\right]^{1 / 2} \\&=\left[\frac{2 \times 9.81 \times(\pi / 4)^{2} \times 0.85^{5}}{0.025 \times 2000}(650-648.5)\right]^{1 / 2}=\underline{0.401 m ^{3} / s} .\end{aligned}\)

 

Apply energy eqn. from loc. 2 to lower reservoir (loc. 3) and determine \(H_{T}\):

 

\(\begin{aligned}H_{T} &=z_{2}-z_{3}-\left(f \frac{L}{D}+K_{v}\right) \frac{Q^{2}}{2 g A^{2}} \\&=648.5-595-\left(\frac{0.02 \times 100}{0.85}+1\right) \frac{0.401^{2}}{2 \times 9.81 \times\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)^{2} \times 0.85^{5}}=\underline{53.4 m }.\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\therefore \dot{W}_{T}=2 Q H_{T} \eta_{T}=9810 \times 0.401 \times 53.4 \times 0.9=\underline{1.89 \times 10^{5} W } \text {, or } \underline{189 kW }\).

 

\(\therefore\) From Fig. 12.32, use a \(\underline{\text {Francis turbine}}\).

A representative value of the specific speed is 2 (Fig. 12.20):

 

\(\therefore \omega=\frac{\Omega_{T}\left(g H_{T}\right)^{5 / 4}}{\left(\dot{W}_{T} / \rho\right)^{1 / 2}}=\frac{2(9.81 \times 53.3)^{5 / 4}}{\left(2.67 \times 10^{5} / 1000\right)^{1 / 2}}=306 rad / s\)

 

or \(N=306 \times 30 / \pi=\underline{2920 rpm}\).
