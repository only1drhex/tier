Guest Question.

The beam shown in Fig. 17.12 is simply supported at nodes 1 and 2 and is attached to a roller support at node 3, which prevents horizontal and rotational displacements. If the flexural rigidity of the beam is EI and it carries a vertical load W at node 3, find the rotation of the beam at nodes 1 and 2, the vertical displacement at node 3, and hence the reactions at the supports. Finally sketch the bending moment diagram for the beam showing the principal values.

Admin
Solution

From Eq. (17.31),

\(\left\{\begin{array}{c}F_{y, i} \\M_{i} \\F_{y, j} \\M_{j}\end{array}\right\}=E I\left[\begin{array}{cccc}12 / L^{3} & -6 / L^{2} & -12 / L^{3} & -6 / L^{2} \\-6 / L^{2} & 4 / L & 6 / L^{2} & 2 / L \\-12 / L^{3} & 6 / L^{2} & 12 / L^{3} & 6 / L^{2} \\-6 / L^{2} & 2 / L & 6 / L^{2} & 4 / L\end{array}\right]\left\{\begin{array}{l}v_{i} \\\theta_{i} \\v_{j} \\\theta_{j}\end{array}\right\}\) (17.31)

the stiffness matrices for the beam are

\(\left[K_{12}\right]=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left[\begin{array}{cccc}12 & -6 L & -12 & -6 L \\-6 L & 4 L^{2} & 6 L & 2 L^{2} \\-12 & 6 L & 12 & 6 L \\-6 L & 2 L^{2} & 6 L & 4 L^{2}\end{array}\right]\) (i)

\(\left[K_{23}\right]=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left[\begin{array}{lccc}96 & -24 L & -96 & -24 L \\-24 L & 8 L^{2} & 24 L & 4 L^{2} \\-96 & 24 L & 96 & 24 L \\-24 L & 4 L^{2} & 24 L & 8 L^{2}\end{array}\right]\) (ii)

Combining Eqs (i) and (ii) and referring to Eq. (17.31) for the complete beam

\(\left[\begin{array}{c}F_{y, 1} \\M_{1}=0 \\F_{y, 2} \\M_{2}=0 \\F_{y, 3}=-W \\M_{3}\end{array}\right]=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left[\begin{array}{cccccc}12 & -6 L & -12 & -6 L & 0 & 0 \\-6 L & 4 L^{2} & 6 L & 2 L^{2} & 0 & 0 \\-12 & 6 L & 108 & -18 L & -96 & -24 L \\-6 L & 2 L^{2} & -18 L & 12 L^{2} & 24 L & 4 L^{2} \\0 & 0 & -96 & 24 L & 96 & 24 L \\0 & 0 & -24 L & 4 L^{2} & 24 L & 8 L^{2}\end{array}\right]\left[\begin{array}{c}v_{1}=0 \\\theta_{1} \\v_{2}=0 \\\theta_{2} \\v_{3} \\\theta_{3}=0\end{array}\right]\) (iii)

Then

\(M_{1}=0=4 L^{2} \theta_{1}+2 L^{2} \theta_{2}\)

so that

\(\theta_{1}=-\frac{\theta_{2}}{2}\) (iv)

Also

\(M_{2}=0=2 L^{2} \theta_{1}+12 L^{2} \theta_{2}+24 L v_{3}\),

which gives

\(v_{3}=-\frac{11}{24} \theta_{2} L\) (v)

Further

\(F_{y, 3}=-W=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left(24 L \theta_{2}+96 v_{3}\right)\) (vi)

Substituting for \(v_{3}\) from Eq. (v) in Eq. (vi) gives

\(\theta_{2}=\frac{W L^{2}}{20 E I}=0.05 \frac{W L^{2}}{E I}\)

Then, from Eq. (iv)

\(\theta_{1}=-0.025 \frac{W L^{2}}{E I}\)

and, from Eq. (v)

\(v_{3}=-0.023 \frac{W L^{3}}{E I}\)

From Eqs (iii)

\(F_{y, 1}=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left(-6 L \theta_{1}-6 L \theta_{2}\right)=-0.15 W\)

 

\(F_{y, 2}=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left(6 L \theta_{1}-18 L \theta_{2}-96 v_{3}\right)=1.15 W\)

and

\(M_{3}=\frac{E I}{L^{3}}\left(4 L^{2} \theta_{2}+24 L v_{3}\right)=-0.35 W L\)

The bending moment diagram is shown in Fig. 17.13.
