Guest Question.

A pair of identical straight-tooth miter gears listed in a catalog has a diametral pitch of 5 at the large end, 25 teeth, a 1.10-in face width, and a 20° normal pressure angle; the gears are grade 1 steel through-hardened with a core and case hardness of 180 Brinell. The gears are uncrowned and intended for general industrial use.
They have a quality number of\( Q_{v}\) = 7. It is likely that the application intended will require outboard mount-ing of the gears. Use a safety factor of 1, a \(10^{7}\) cycle life, and a 0.99 reliability.
(a) For a speed of 600 rev/min find the power rating of this gearset based on AGMA bending strength.
(b) For the same conditions as in part (a) find the power rating of this gearset based on AGMA wear strength.

(c) For a reliability of 0.995, a gear life of \(10^{9}\) revolutions, and a safety factor of \(S_{F} = S_{H} =\) 1.5, find the power rating for this gearset using AGMA strengths.

Admin
Solution

From Figs. 15–14 and 15–15,

\( d_{P} = N_{P}∕P_{d} = 25∕5 = 5.000 \) in

\(v_{t} = πd_{P}n_{P}∕12 = π(5) 600∕12 = 785.4 ft/min \)

 

Overload factor: uniform-uniform loading, Table 15–2, \(K_{o}\) = 1.00.
Safety factor: \(S_{F} = 1, S_{H} = 1.\) Dynamic factor\( K_{v}\): from Eq. (15–6),\(A = 50 + 56(1 − B) \)
B\( = 0.25(12 − Q_{v})^{2/3} \)

\( B = 0.25(12 − 7)^{2∕3} = 0.731\)

A = 50 + 56(1 − 0.731) = 65.06

\(K_{v} = (\frac{65.06 + \sqrt{785.4}}{65.06} )^{0.731}= 1.299 \)

From Eq. (15–8),\( v_{t max }= [A + (Q_{v} − 3)]^{2}\) (U.S. customary units)

\(v_{t max} = [65.06 + (7 − 3)]^{2} = 4769 \)ft/min

\(v_{t} < v_{t max}\), that is, 785.4 < 4769 ft/min, therefore\( K_{v} \)is valid. From Eq. (15–10),

:\( K_{S} =\begin{cases} 0.4867 + 0.2132∕P_{d}&0.5 ≤ P_{d} ≤ 16 teeth/in\\0.5&P_{d} > 16 teeth/in\end{cases}\)

 

\(K_{S}= \begin{cases} 0.4867 + 0.2132/P_{d} & 0.5 ≤ Pd ≤ 16 teeth/in \\ 0.5 & P_{d} > 16 teeth/in \end{cases}\) \(K_{s} = 0.4867 + 0.2132∕5 = 0.529\)

From Eq. (15–11),\(K_{m} = K_{mb} + 0.0036 F^{2}\) (U.S. customary units)
\(K_{Hβ} = K_{mb} + 5.6(10^{−6})b^{2}\) (SI units)

\(K_{mb} = 1.25 \)and \( K_{m} = 1.25 + 0.0036(1.10)^{2} = 1.254\)

From Eq. (15–13)\( K_{x} = Y_{β} = 1\), \(K_{x} =\) 1. From Fig. 15–6, I = 0.065; from Fig. 15–7, \(J_{P} = 0.216, J_{G}\) = 0.216. From Eq. (15–15),

\( K_{L}=\begin{cases} 2.7 &10^{2} ≤ N_{L} < 10^{3} \\ 6.1514N^{−0.1192}_{L}& 10^{3} ≤ N_{L} ≤ 3(10^{6})\\1.683N^{−0.0323}_{L}&3(10^{6}) ≤ N_{L} ≤ 10^{10} &critical \\1.3558N^{−0.0178}_{L}&3(10^{6}) ≤ N_{L} ≤ 10^{10}&general \end{cases} \)

 

\(Y_{NT}=\begin{cases} 2.7 &10^{2} ≤ n_{L} < 10^{3} \\ 6.1514n^{−0.1192}_{L}& 10^{3} ≤ n_{L} ≤ 3(10^{6})\\1.683n^{−0.0323}_{L}&3(10^{6}) ≤ n_{L} ≤ 10^{10} &critical \\1.3558n^{−0.0178}_{L}&3(10^{6}) ≤ n_{L} ≤ 10^{10}&general \end{cases} \)

 

\(K_{L} = 1.683(10^{7})^{−0.0323} = 0.999 96 ≈ 1\)

From Eq. (15–14),

\( C_{L}=\begin{cases}2 &10^{3} ≤ NL < 10^{4} \\3.4822N^{−0.0602}_{L}& 10^{4} ≤ N_{L} ≤ 10^{10}\end{cases} \)

 

\( Z_{NT}=\begin{cases}2 &10^{3} ≤ nL < 10^{4} \\3.4822n^{−0.0602}_{L}& 10^{4} ≤ n_{L} ≤ 10^{10}\end{cases} \)

 

\(C_{L} = 3.4822(10^{7})^{−0.0602 }= 1.32\)

Since \(H_{BP}∕H_{BG} \)= 1, then from Fig. 15–10,\( C_{H} =\) 1. From Eqs. (15–13) \( K_{x} = Y_{β} = 1 \) and (15–18)

\(K_{T}=\begin{cases} 1&32°F ≤ t ≤ 250°F\\(460 + t)∕710&t > 250°F\end{cases} \)

 

\(K_{θ} =\begin{cases}1&0°C ≤ θ ≤ 120°C\\(273 + θ)∕393&θ > 120°C\end{cases}\)

, \(K_{x}\) = 1 and\( K_{T}\) = 1, respectively. From Eq. (15–20),

\( Y_{Z} = K_{R} =\begin{cases} 0.50 − 0.25 log(1 − R) & 0.99 ≤ R ≤ 0.999 \\0.70 − 0.15 log(1 − R) & 0.90 ≤ R < 0.99 \end{cases} \)

 

\(K_{R} = 0.70 − 0.15 log(1 − 0.99) = 1, C_{R} = \sqrt{K_{R}} = sqrt{1} = 1\)

(a) Bending: From Eq. (15–23),

\(\begin{matrix} s_{at}=44H_{B}+2100psi & (15–23) \\ \sigma _{F lim}=0.30H_{B}+14.48MPa \\s_{at}=48H_{B}+5980psi \\\sigma _{H lim}=0.33H_{B}+41.24MPa \end{matrix} \) \(s_{at} = 44(180) + 2100 = 10 020 psi\)

From Eq. (15–3) \(\begin{matrix} s_{t}=\frac{W^{t}}{F}P_{d}K_{0}K_{v}\frac{K_{s}K_{m}}{K_{x}J} &(U.S. customary units) & (15–3) \\ \sigma _{F}=\frac{1000W^{t}}{b} \frac{K_{A}K_{v}}{m_{et}} \frac{Y_{x}K_{H\beta }}{Y_{\beta }Y_{J}} & (SI units) \end{matrix}\), \( s_{at} = 44H_{B} + 2100 psi\) grade 1

\(s_{t} = σ = \frac{W^{t}}{F}P_{d}K_{o}K_{v}\frac{K_{s}K_{m}}{K_{x} J} =\frac{ W^{t}}{1.10} (5)(1)1.299\frac{0.529(1.254)}{(1)0.216} \)

= \(18.13W^{t}\)

From Eq. (15–4) \(\begin{matrix} s_{wt}=\frac{s_{at}K_{L}}{S_{F}K_{T}K_{R}} &(U.S. customary units) & (15–4) \\ \sigma _{FP}=\frac{\sigma _{F lim}Y_{NT}}{S_{F}K_{0}Y_{z}} & (SI units) \end{matrix} \),

\(s_{wt} = \frac{s_{at}K_{L}}{S_{F}K_{T}K_{R}}=\frac{ 10 020(1)}{(1)(1)(1)} = 10 020 psi \)

Equating \(s_{t } and s_{wt},\)

18.13\(W^{ t} = 10 020 W^{ t} = 552.6\) lbf

\(H =\frac{ W^{t}v_{t}}{33 000} =\frac{ 552.6(785.4)}{33 000} = 13.2 hp \)

(b) Wear: From Fig. 15–12 or Eq. (15–22),

\(s_{ac} \)= 341(180) + 23 620 = 85 000 psi

From Eq. (15–2),

\(σ_{c},_{all} =\frac {s_{ac}C_{L}C_{H}}{S_{H}K_{T}C_{R}}=\frac{ 85 000(1.32)(1)}{(1)(1)(1)} = 112 200 psi \)

Now \(C_{p} = 2290\sqrt{psi}\) from definitions following Eq. (15–21). From Eq. (15–9),

\(\begin{matrix} C_{s}= \begin{cases} 0.5 & F<0.5in \\ 0.125F+0.4375 & 0.5\leq F\lneq 4.5in \\1 & F>4.5 in \end{cases} & (U.S. customary units) \\ Z_{x}= \begin{cases} 0.5 & b<12.7mm \\ 0.00492b+0.4375 & 12.7\leq b\leq 114.3 mm \\1 &b>114.3 mm \end{cases} & (SI units) \end{matrix} \) Eq. (15–9)

\(C_{s} = 0.125(1.1) + 0.4375 = 0.575\)

From Eq. (15–12)\( C_{xc} = Z_{xc} = \begin{cases} 1.5&properly crowned teeth\\2.0&or larger uncrowned teeth \end{cases}\) ,\( C_{xc} = 2.\) Substituting in Eq. (15–1) gives

\(σ_{c} = C_{p}(\frac{W ^{t}}{Fd_{P}I}K_{o}K_{v}K_{m}C_{s}C_{xc})^{1∕2} \) \( = 2290[\frac{W^{t}}{1.10(5)0.065} (1)1.299(1.254)0.575(2)]^{1∕2}= 5242\sqrt{W^{t}} \)

Equating\( σ_{c} \) and \(σ_{c},_{all}\) gives

5242\(\sqrt{W^{t}} = 112 200, W^{t} = 458.1 \)lbf

\( H =\frac{ 458.1(785.4)}{33 000 }= 10.9 hp \)

Rated power for the gearset is

Answer H = min(12.9, 10.9) = 10.9 hp

(c) Life goal \(10^{9} \)cycles, R = 0.995,\( S_{F} = S_{H} \)= 1.5, and from Eq.(15–15),

\(K_{L} = 1.683(10^{9})^{−0.0323} =\) 0.8618

From Eq. (15–19) \(Y_{z}=K_{R}= \begin{cases} \begin{matrix} 0.50-0.25 log (1-R) & 0.99\leq R\leq 0.999 &(15–19) \\ 0.70-0.15 log (1-R) & 0.90 \leq R \leq 0.99 &(15–20) \end{matrix} \end{cases} \),

\( K_{R} = 0.50 − 0.25 log(1 − 0.995) = 1.075, C_{R} = \sqrt{K_{R}} = \sqrt{1.075} = 1.037 \)

From Eq. (15–14),

\( C_{L} = 3.4822(10^{9})^{−0.0602} = 1 \)

Bending: From Eq. (15–23) and part (a),\( s_{at}\) = 10 020 psi. From Eq. (15–3),

\( s_{t} = σ = \frac{W^{t}}{1.10}5(1)1.299\frac{0.529(1.254)}{(1)0.216} = 18.13W^{t} \)

From Eq. (15–4),

\( s_{wt} =\frac{ s_{at}K_{L}}{S_{F}K_{T}K_{R}}= \frac{10 020(0.8618)}{1.5(1)1.075} = 5355 psi \)

18.13\(W^{t} = 5355 W^{t} = 295.4\) lbf

\( H =\frac{ 295.4(785.4)}{33 000} = 7.0 hp \)

\(\begin{matrix} s_{ac}=341H_{B}+23620psi & grade 1 & (15–22) \\ \sigma _{H lim}=2.35H_{B}+162.89MPa &grade 1 \\s_{ac}=363.6H_{B}+29 560psi &grade 2 \\\sigma _{H lim}=2.51H_{B}+203.86MPa &grade 2 \end{matrix} \)

Substituting into Eq. (15–2) gives

\( σ_{c},_{all} =\frac{ s_{ac}C_{L}C_{H}}{S_{H}K_{T}C_{R}}=\frac{ 85 000(1)(1)}{1.5(1)1.037} = 54 640 psi \)

From part (b), σ\(_{c} = 5242\sqrt{W^{t}}\) and equating \(σ_{c} \)to \(σ_{c},_{all}\) gives

\( σ_{c} = σ_{c},_{all} = 54 640 = 5242\sqrt{W^{t}} W^{t} = 108.6 lbf \)

The wear power is

\( H = \frac{108.6(785.4)}{33 000} = 2.58 hp \)
