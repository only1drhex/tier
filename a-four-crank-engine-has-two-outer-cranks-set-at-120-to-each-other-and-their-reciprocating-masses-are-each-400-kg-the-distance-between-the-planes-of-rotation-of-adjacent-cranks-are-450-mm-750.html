Guest Question.

A four crank engine has two outer cranks set at 120° to each other, and their reciprocating masses are each 400 kg. The distance between the planes of rotation of adjacent cranks are 450 mm, 750 mm, and 600 mm. If the engine is to be in complete primary balance, find the reciprocating mass and the relative angular position for each of the inner cranks.
If the length of each cranks is 300 mm, length of each connecting rod is 1.2 m and the speed of rotation is 240 rpm, what is the maximum secondary unbalanced force?
