Guest Question.

For the NAVION airplane described in Appendix B, estimate the roll control power, \(C_{I_{8_a}}\) . Assume that the wing and aileron geometry are as shown in Figure 2.37.

Admin
Solution

Equation (2.96) can be used to estimate the roll control power, \(C_{I_{8_a}}\)

\(C_{I_{8_a}}\frac{2C_{L_{a_w}}\tau}{Sb} \int_{y_1}^{y_2}{cy dy}\)
b/2 = 16.7 ft. λ = 0.54 \(c_r\) =7.2 ft.
\(c_t\) = 3.9\( ft. y_1 = 11.1 ft. y_2 = 16 ft.\)
S = 184\( ft^2. C_{L_{a_w}}\) = 4.44/rad \(c_a/c\) = 0.18 ft.
For a tapered wing the chord can be expressed as a function of y by the following relationship:
\(c=c_r[1+(\frac{\lambda -1}{b/2})y]\)
Substituting the relationship for the chord back into the expression for \(C_{I_{8_a}}\) yields
\(C_{I_{8_a}}\frac{2C_{L_{a_w}}\tau C_r}{Sb} \int_{y_1}^{y_2}{[1+(\frac{\lambda -1}{b/2})y]y dy}\)
or \(C_{I_{8_a}}\frac{2C_{L_{a_w}}\tau C_r}{Sb}[\frac{y^2}{2}+(\frac{\lambda -1}{b/2})\frac{y^3}{3}]\mid _{y_1}^{y_2}\)
This equation can be used to estimate \(C_{I_{8_a}}\), using the data in Figure 2.37 and estimating
τ from Figure 2.21. Because the chord ratio is the same as the area ratio used in
Figure 2.21, we can use \(c_a/c\) = 0.18 to estimate the flap effectiveness parameter, τ
\(C_{I_{8_a}}=\frac{2(4.3/rad)(0.36)(7.2 ft)}{(184 ft^2)(33.4ft)}(90.4 ft^2 – 49 ft^2)\)
= 0.155/rad
The control derivative \(C_{I_{8_a}}\) is a measure of the power of the aileron control; it represents the change in moment per unit of aileron deflection. The larger \(C_{I_{8_a}}\), the more effective
the control is at producing a roll moment.
