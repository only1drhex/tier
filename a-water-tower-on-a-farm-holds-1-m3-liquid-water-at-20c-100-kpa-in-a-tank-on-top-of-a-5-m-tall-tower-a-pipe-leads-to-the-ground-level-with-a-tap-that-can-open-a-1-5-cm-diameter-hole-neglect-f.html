Guest Question.

A water tower on a farm holds 1 \(m ^{3}\) liquid water at 20째C, 100 kPa in a tank on top of a 5 m tall tower. A pipe leads to the ground level with a tap that can open a 1.5 cm diameter hole. Neglect friction and pipe losses, and estimate the time it will take to empty the tank for water.

Admin
Solution

Incompressible flow so we can use Bernoulli Equation.

\(P _{ e }= P _{ i } ; \quad V _{ i }=0 ; \quad Z _{ e }=0 ; \quad Z _{ i }= H\)

 

\(V _{ e }^{2} / 2= g Z_{ i } \Rightarrow \quad V _{ e }=\sqrt{2 gZ }=\sqrt{2 \times 9.807 \times 5}=9.9 m / s\)

 

\(\dot{ m }=\rho A V _{ e }= A V _{ e } / v =\Delta m / \Delta t\)

 

\(\begin{aligned}&\Delta m = V / v ; \quad A =\pi D ^{2} / 4=\pi \times 0.015^{2} / 4=1.77 \times 10^{-4} m ^{2} \\&\Rightarrow \quad \Delta t =\Delta mv / A V _{ e }= V / A V _{ e }\end{aligned}\)

 

\(\Delta t =\frac{1}{1.77 \times 10^{-4} \times 9.9}= 5 7 1 . 6 sec =9.53 min\)
