Guest Question.

Water flows through the Tee fitting at \( 0.02 m^3/s.\) If the pipe at B is extended and the pressure in the pipe at B is 75 kPa, determine the horizontal and vertical components of force, and the moment that must be exerted on the fixed support at A, to hold the fitting in equilibrium. Neglect the weight of the fitting and the water within it.

Admin
Solution

The flow is steady and the water can be considered as an ideal fluid (incompressible and inviscid) such that \(\rho _w = 1000 kg/m^3. \)Average velocities will be used. The

control volume consists of the fitting, fixed support and the contained water. From the discharge,

\(Q = V_CA_C; 0.02 m^3/s = V_C[\pi (0.05 m)^2] V_C = 2.546 m/s \)

 

\(Q = V_BA_B; 0.02 m^3/s = V_B[\pi(0.03 m)^2] V_B = 7.074 m/s \)

Applying Bernoulli’s equation between A and B,

\(\frac{P_C}{\gamma _w} + \frac{V_C^2}{2g} + z_C = \frac{P_B}{\gamma _w} + \frac{V_B^2}{2g} + z_B \)

 

\(\frac{P_C}{9810 N/m^3} + \frac{(2.546 m/s)^2}{2(9.81 m/s^2)} + 0 = \frac{75(10^3) N/m^2}{9810 N/m^3} + \frac{(7.074 m/s)^2}{2(9.81 m/s^2)}+ 0 \)

 

\(p_C = 96.775(10^3) N/m^2 \)

Then the pressure forces on the inlet and outlet control surfaces at C and B are

\(F_C = p_CA_C = [96.775(10^3) N/m^2] [\pi (0.05 m)^2] = 760.07 N \)

 

\(F_B = p_BA_B = [75(10^3) N/m^2] [\pi (0.03 m)^2] = 212.06 N \)

Applying linear momentum equation,

\(\sum F = \frac{\partial }{\partial t}\int_{cv} V\rho_w dV + \int_{cs} V \rho_w V \cdot dA\)

writing the scalar component of this equation along the x and y axes by referring to the free-body diagram, Fig. a,

\((\overset{+}{\rightarrow} ) \sum F_x = 0 + V_A\rho _w( -V_AA_A) \)

 

\(A_x + 760.07 N = (2.546 m/s)(1000 kg/m^3)( -0.02 m^3/s) \)

 

\(A_x = -811 N = 811 Nd \)

 

\(( + ↑)\sum F_y = 0 + V_B\rho _w(V_BA_B) \)

 

\(A_y – 212.06 N = (7.074 m/s)(1000 kg/m^3)(0.02 m^3/s) \)

 

\(A_y = 353.53 N = 354 Nc \)

Applying the angular momentum equation,

\(\sum M = \frac{\partial }{\partial t} \int_{cv}(r × V ) \rho_w dV + \int_{cs}(r × V) \rho_w V \cdot dA \)

writing the scalar component of this equation about point A by referring to Fig. a,

\(↓ + \sum M_A = 0 + r_BV_B\rho _w(V_BA_B) – r_CV_C \rho _w( -V_CA_C) \)

 

\(M_A – (760.07 N)(0.2 m) – (212.06 N)(0.15 m) = (0.15 m)(7.074 m/s)(1000 kg/m^3)(0.02 m^3/s) – (0.2 m)(2.546 m/s)(1000 kg/m^3)( -0.02 m^3/s) \)

 

\(M_A = 215.22 N \cdot m = 215 N \cdot m \)
