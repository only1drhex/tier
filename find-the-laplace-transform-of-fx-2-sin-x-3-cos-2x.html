Guest Question.

Find the Laplace transform of f(x) = 2 sin x + 3 cos 2x .

Admin
Solution

Using Property 21.1 with entries 8 (a = 1) and 9 (a = 2) of Appendix A, we have

\(\begin{aligned}F(s) &= \mathscr{L} \{2 \sin x+3 \cos 2 x\}=2 \mathscr{L} \{\sin x\}+3 \mathscr{L} \{\cos 2 x\} \\&=2 \frac{1}{s^{2}+1}+3 \frac{s}{s^{2}+4}=\frac{2}{s^{2}+1}+\frac{3 s}{s^{2}+4}\end{aligned}\)
