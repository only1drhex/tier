In the production of silicon wafers, 30 lots of size 500 are sampled, and the proportion of defective wafers is calculated for each sample. Table \(10.2\) presents the results. Solution

The average of the 30 sample proportions is \(\bar{p}=0.050867\). The center line is therefore plotted at \(0.050867\). The control limits are plotted at \(0.050867 \pm\) \(3 \sqrt{(0.050867)(0.949133) / 500}\). The upper control limit is therefore \(0.0803\), and the lower control limit is \(0.0214\). Figure \(10.14\) presents the \(p\) chart. The process appears to be in control.
