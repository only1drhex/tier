Guest Question.

The properties of a composite material are known to be

\(E_{11}=25 Msi E_{22} = E_{33} = 1.5 Msi\)

 

\(ν_{12} = ν_{13} =0.30 ν_{23}=0.40\)

 

\(G_{12}=G_{13}=2.0 Msi\)

Note that only five distinct material properties have been specified, indicating that this composite material is transversely isotropic. Determine the strains caused by the following state of stress:

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left \{ \begin{matrix} 50 ksi \\ 5 ksi \\ 2 ksi \\ 4 ksi \\1.5 ksi \\3.5 ksi \end{matrix} \right \}\)
Admin
Solution

As the composite is transversely isotropic, strains are calculated using Equation 4.21.Individual terms within the compliance matrix are

\(S_{11}= \frac{1}{E_{11}}= \frac{1}{25 Msi}= \frac{40.0}{10^9 psi}\)

 

\(S_{22}=S_{33}= \frac{1}{E_{22}}= \frac{1}{1.5 Msi}= \frac{667}{10^9 psi}\)

 

\(S_{44}= \frac{1}{G_{23}}= \frac{2(1 + \nu _{23})}{E_{22}}= \frac{2(1 +0.40)}{1.5 Msi}=\frac{1866}{10^9 psi}\)

 

\(S_{55}=S_{66}= \frac{1}{G_{12}}= \frac{1}{2.0Msi}= \frac{500}{10^9 psi}\)

 

\(S_{12}=S_{21}=S_{13}=S_{31} = \frac{- \nu _{12}}{E_{11}}= \frac{- 0.30}{25 Msi}= \frac{- 12.0}{10^9 psi}\)

 

\(S_{23}=S_{32} = \frac{- \nu _{23}}{E_{22}}= \frac{- 0.40}{1.5 Msi}= \frac{- 266}{10^9 psi}\)

Equation 4.16

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \varepsilon _{33} \\ \gamma _{23} \\ \gamma _{13} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left [ \begin{matrix} S_{11} & S_{12} & S_{13} & 0 &0 & 0 \\ S_{12} & S_{22} &S_{23} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ S_{13} & S_{23} & S_{33} &0 &0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & S_{44} & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &S_{55} & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &S_{66} \end{matrix} \right ]\left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}\) (4.16)

becomes

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \varepsilon _{33} \\ \gamma _{23} \\ \gamma _{13} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left [ \begin{matrix} 40.0 & – 12.0 & – 12.0 & 0 &0 & 0 \\ -12.0 & 667.0 &-266.0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\ -12.0 & -266.0 & 667.0 &0 &0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 800.0 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &500.0 & 0 \\0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 &500.0 \end{matrix} \right ]\left\lgroup\frac{1}{10^9 psi} \right\rgroup \left\{\begin{matrix} \sigma _{11} \\ \sigma _{22} \\ \sigma _{33} \\ \tau _{23} \\ \tau _{13} \\ \tau _{12} \end{matrix} \right\} (10^3 psi)\)

 

\(\left\{\begin{matrix} \varepsilon _{11} \\ \varepsilon _{22} \\ \varepsilon _{33} \\ \gamma _{23} \\ \gamma _{13} \\ \gamma _{12} \end{matrix} \right\}=\left \{ \begin{matrix} 1916 μin./in. \\2203μin./in. \\ -596μin./in. \\ 3200μrat \\ 750μrat \\ 1750μrat \end{matrix} \right \}\)
