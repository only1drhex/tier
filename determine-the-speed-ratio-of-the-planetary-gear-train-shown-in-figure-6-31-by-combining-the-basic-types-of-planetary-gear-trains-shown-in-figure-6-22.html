The following equations are generated by considering two specified input rotational speeds, one of which is zero, and constraints imposed by the connected components of the planetary gear train.

\((ω_{1})_{1} = ω_{input} = ω_{1}\) (6.5-13)

\(ω_{6} = (ω_{5})_{1} = 0\) (6.5-14)

\((ω_{1})_{1} = (ω_{1})_{2} \) (6.5-15)

\((ω_{2})_{1} = (ω_{2})_{2} \) (6.5-16)

\((ω_{3})_{1} = (ω_{3})_{2} \) (6.5-17)

Expressing Equations (6.5-13)–(6.5-17) in terms of the unknowns given in Figure 6.22, we obtain

\(x_{1} + y_{1} = ω_{1}\) (6.5-18)

\( x_{1} – \left\lgroup\frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{5}} \right\rgroup _{1} y_{1} = 0 \) or \( x_{1} – \frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{6}} y_{1} = 0 \) (6.5-19)

\(x_{1} + y_{1} = x_{2} + y_{2} \) (6.5-20)

\(x_{1} = x_{2} \) (6.5-21)

\(x_{1} – \frac{N_{1}}{N_{3}} y_{1} = x_{2} – \frac{N_{1}}{N_{3}} y_{2}\) (6.5-22)

However, Equation (6.5-22) is a linear combination of Equations (6.5-20) and (6.5-21). Therefore, we only need to consider solving Equations (6.5-18)–(6.5-21) with unknowns \(x_{1}, x_{2}, y_{1}\), and \(y_{2}\), and then Equation (6.5-22) will be automatically satisfied.
Combining Equations (6.5-18)–(6.5-21) in matrix form, we get

\(\left[\begin{matrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 &\left\lgroup- \frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{6}}\right\rgroup & 0 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & -1& -1 \\ 1 & 0 & -1 & 0 \end{matrix} \right] \left\{\begin{matrix} x_{1} \\ y_{1} \\ x_{2} \\ y_{2} \end{matrix} \right\} = \left\{\begin{matrix} ω_{1} \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{matrix} \right\}\) (6.5-23)

Solving Equation (6.5-23) gives

\( x_{1} = x_{2} = ω_{1} \frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{6} + N_{1} N_{4}}\) (6.5-24)

\( y_{1} = y_{2} = ω_{1} \frac{N_{3} N_{6}}{N_{3} N_{6} + N_{1} N_{4}}\) (6.5-25)

The output rotational speed of the gear train is

\(ω_{7} = (ω_{5})_{2} = x_{2} – \left\lgroup\frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{5}} \right\rgroup_{2} y_{2}\) or \(ω_{7} = x_{2} – \frac{N_{1} N_{5}}{N_{3} N_{7}} y_{2} \) (6.5-26)

Substituting Equations (6.5-24) and (6.5-25) in (6.5-26),we obtain

\(ω_{7} = ω_{1} \frac{N_{1} N_{4}}{N_{3} N_{6} + N_{1} N_{4} } – \frac{N_{1} N_{5}}{N_{3} N_{7} } ω_{1} \frac{N_{3} N_{6}}{N_{3} N_{6} + N_{1} N_{4} } \) (6.5-27)

The speed ratio of the gear train is obtained from Equation (6.5-27) and yields after simplification

\(e_{7/1} = \frac{ω_{7} }{ω_{1} } = \frac{1- \left\lgroup\frac{N_{6} N_{5}}{N_{4} N_{7}} \right\rgroup }{1+ \left\lgroup\frac{N_{6} N_{3}}{N_{4} N_{1}} \right\rgroup } \) (6.5-28)
