Guest Question.

A propped cantilever beam is loaded as shown in Fig. P11.19. Assume EI = 200,000 \(kN - m ^{2}\) . Use discontinuity functions to determine:
(a) the reactions at A and B.
(b) the beam deflection at C.

Admin
Solution

Moment equation:
(a) Support reactions:

\(\begin{aligned}&\Sigma F_{y}=A_{y}+B_{y}=0 \\&\therefore A_{y}=-B_{y} (a)\\&\Sigma M_{A}=-750 kN – m +B_{y}(5 m )-M_{A}=0 \\&\therefore M_{A}-B_{y}(5 m )=-750 kN – m (b)\end{aligned}\)

Discontinuity expressions:

\(\begin{array}{r}w(x)=M_{A}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{-2}+A_{y}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{-1}+B_{y}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{-1}+750 kN – m \langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{-2} \\V(x)=\int w(x) d x=M_{A}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{-1}+A_{y}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{0}+B_{y}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{0}+750 kN – m \langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{-1} \\M(x)=\int V(x) d x=M_{A}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{0}+A_{y}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{1}+B_{y}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{1}+750 kN – m \langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{0} \\E I \frac{d^{2} v}{d x^{2}}=M(x)=M_{A}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{0}+A_{y}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{1}+B_{y}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{1}+750 kN – m \langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{0} \\E I \frac{d v}{d x}=M_{A}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{1}+\frac{A_{y}}{2}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{2}+\frac{B_{y}}{2}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{2}+750 kN – m \langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{1}+C_{1} (c) \\E I v=\frac{M_{A}}{2}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{2}+\frac{A_{y}}{6}\langle x-0 m \rangle^{3}+\frac{B_{y}}{6}\langle x-5 m \rangle^{3}+\frac{750 kN – m }{2}\langle x-7.5 m \rangle^{2}+C_{1} x+C_{2} (d)\end{array}\)

 

Boundary conditions and evaluate constants:

\(\begin{array}{ll}\text { at } x=0 m , \frac{d v}{d x}=0 & \therefore C_{1}=0 \\\text { at } x=0 m , v=0 & \therefore C_{2}=0 \\\text { at } x=5 m , v=0 & 0=\frac{M_{A}}{2}(5 m )^{2}+\frac{A_{y}}{6}(5 m )^{3} \\\therefore M_{A}\left(12.5 m ^{2}\right)+A_{y}\left(20.83333 m ^{3}\right)=0 (e)\end{array}\)

 

(a) Solve for \(A _{y}, B _{y}\), and \(M _{A}\):
Solve equations (a), (b), and (c) simultaneously to obtain the results:

\(A_{y}=-225.000 kN =225 kN \downarrow \quad B_{y}=225.000 kN =225 kN \uparrow\)

 

\( M_{A}=375.000 kN – m =375 kN – m ( cw )\)

 

(b) Beam deflection at C: From Eq. (d), the beam deflection at C (x = 7.5 m) is computed as follows:

\(\begin{aligned}E I v_{C} &=\frac{M_{A}}{2}(7.5 m )^{2}+\frac{A_{y}}{6}(7.5 m )^{3}+\frac{B_{y}}{6}(2.5 m )^{3} \\&=-4,687.500 kN – m ^{3} \\& \therefore v_{C}=-\frac{4,687.500 kN – m ^{3}}{200,000 kN – m ^{2}}=-0.023438 m =23.4 mm \downarrow\end{aligned}\)
