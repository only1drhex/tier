Solution

\( \text { The complete combustion of propane follows } C _{3} H _{8}+5 O _{2} \rightarrow 3 CO _{2}+4 H _{2} O ( g ) \text {. The surrounding water sustains } \) a constant-temperature process, so that (13.11) becomes

\( Q=\sum_{\text {prod }} N_{i}\left(\bar{h}_{f}^{\circ}+\bar{h}-\bar{h}^{\circ}-R_{u} T\right)_{i}-\sum_{\text {react }} N_{i}\left(\bar{h}_{f}^{\circ}+\bar{h}-\bar{h}^{\circ}-R_{u} T\right)_{i} \) (13.11).

\( Q=\sum_{\text {nmd }} N_{i}\left(\bar{h}_{f}^{\circ}\right)_{i}-\sum N_{i}\left(\bar{h}_{f}^{\circ}\right)_{i}+\left(N_{R}-N_{P}\right) R_{u} T=-874,000 \).
