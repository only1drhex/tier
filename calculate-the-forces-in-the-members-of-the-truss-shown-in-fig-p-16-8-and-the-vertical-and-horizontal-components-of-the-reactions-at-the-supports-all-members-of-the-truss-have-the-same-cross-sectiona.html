Guest Question.

Calculate the forces in the members of the truss shown in Fig. P.16.8 and the vertical and horizontal components of the reactions at the supports; all members of the truss have the same cross-sectional properties.
Ans. \(R_{ A , V }=67.52 kN , R_{ A , H }=70.06 kN =R_{ F , H }, R_{ F , V }=32.48 kN\)
AB = -32.48 kN, AD = -78.31 kN, BC = -64.98 kN, BD = 72.65 kN,
CD = -100.0 kN, CE = -64.98 kN, DE = 72.65 kN, DF = -70.06 kN,
EF = -32.49 kN.

Admin
Solution

In this problem it is the support system which is statically indeterminate. Therefore release the truss horizontally at F. Then, from Table S.16.8

Σ=-1292.53 Σ=18.45
\(-1292.53+18.45 R_{ F , H }=0\)

from which

\(R_{ F , H }=70.06 kN =R_{ A , H }\)

and

\(R_{ A , V }=50+0.25 \times 70.06=67.52 kN\)

so that, from equilibrium

\(R_{ F , V }=32.48 kN\)

The actual forces F in the members of the truss are calculated as, for example for AB as follows

\(F=-50+0.25 \times 70.06=-32.48 kN\)

and are given in the final column of Table S.16.8.
