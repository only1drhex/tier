Guest Question.

Design a collector coupled astable multivibrator using \(V_{CC(peak)}\) = 20 V and \(I_{c(sat)}\) = 3 mA, to generate a pulse wave at a frequency f = 2 kHz with 70% duty cycle. Assume \(h_{fe(min)}\) = 100.

Admin
Solution

Refer to Fig. 16.63.
Given: \(I_{c(sat)}\) = 3 mA, f = 2 kHz, \(h_{fe(min)}\) = 100 and duty cycle = 70% = 0.7

\(R_{C} =\frac{V_{CC} – V_{CE(sat)}}{I_{C}}=\frac{20 – 0.3}{3 × 10^{– 3}}\) = 6.56 kΩ

Here, \(I_{B} =\frac{I_{C}}{h_{fe}}=\frac{3 × 10^{– 3}}{100}\) = 0.03 mA

\(R_{1} =\frac{V_{CC} – V_{BE(sat)}}{I_{B}}=\frac{20 – 0.7}{0.03 × 10^{– 3}}\) = 643.33 kΩ

\(R_{2} = R_{1}\) = 643.33 kΩ

Here, T = \( \frac{1}{f} =\frac{1}{2 × 10^{3}}\)= 0.5 ms or 500 μs

We know that Duty cycle = \(\frac{T_{ON}}{T_{ON} + T_{OFF}}=\frac{T_{ON}}{T}\)

Therefore, 0.7 = \(\frac{T_{ON}}{500 × 10^{– 6}}\) where \(T_{ON}\) = 350 μs and \(T_{OFF}\) = 150 μs

We know that \(T_{ON} = 0.693 R_{1}C_{1}\)

Therefore, 350 × \(10^{–6} = 0.693 × 643.33 × 10^{3} × C_{1}\)

\(C_{1} =\frac{350 × 10^{– 6}}{0.693 × 643.33 × 10^{3}}\) = 0.785 μF

We know that, \(T_{OFF} = 0.693 R_{2}C_{2}\)

Therefore, 150 × \(10^{– 6} = 0.693 × 643.33 × 10^{3} × C_{2}\)

\(C_{2} =\frac{150 × 10^{– 6}}{0.693 × 643.33 × 10^{3}}\) = 0.336 nF
