Guest Question.

(a) Find the change in volume of \(1.00\, ft^{3}\) of water at 80°F when subjected to a pressure increase of 300 psi.

(b) From the following test data determine the bulk modulus of elasticity of water : at 500 psi the volume was \(1.00\, ft^{3}\) ,and at 3500 psi the volume was \(0.990\, ft^{3} \).

Admin
