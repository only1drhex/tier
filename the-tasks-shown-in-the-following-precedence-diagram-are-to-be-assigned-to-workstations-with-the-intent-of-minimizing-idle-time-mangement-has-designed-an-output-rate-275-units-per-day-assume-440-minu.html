a. Determine the appropriate cycle time. b. what is the minimum number of satations possible?

b. what is the minimum number of statios possible ?

a. Cycle time = \(\frac{Operating time}{Desired output}= \frac{440 minutes per day}{275 units per day} = 1.6\) minutes

b. \(N =\frac{\sum{t} }{Cycle time}= \frac{4.2}{1.6 minutes}= 2.625\) (round to 3)
