Solution

For making the components inhouse, the AOC is comprised of direct labor, direct material, and indirect costs. Use the data of Table 15-6 to calculate the indirect cost allocation.

Department A: 25 ,000(10) = $250,000

Department B: 25 ,000(5) = 125,000

Department C: 10,000(15) = \(\underset{\$525,000}{\underline{150,000} } \)

AOC = 500,000 + 300,000 + 525,000 = $1,325,000

The make alternative annual worth is the total of capital recovery and AOC.

\(AW_{make} = -P(A/P,i ,n) + S(A/F,i ,n) – AOC\)
\(= -2,000,000(A/ P,15\%,10) + 50,000(A / F, 15\%,10) – 1,325 ,000\)
\(= \$- 1,721 ,037\)
