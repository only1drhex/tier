\( CO(g)+2H_{2}(g)\rightarrow CH_{3}OH(g)\)
Admin

\( \Delta H^{\circ }=\sum\limits_{i}{\nu _{i}\Delta H^{\circ }_{fi}}\) (4.16)

From its definition,

\( \Delta A=(1)(2.211)+(-1)(3.376)+(-2)(3.249)=-7.663\)

Similarly,

\( \Delta B=10.815\times 10^{-3} \Delta C=-3.450\times 10^{-6} \Delta D=-0.135\times 10^{5}\)

The value of the integral of Eq. (4.20) for \(T = 1,073.15 K\) is represented by:

\(lDCPH(298.15,1073.15;-7.663,10.815\times 10^{-3},-3.450\times 10^{-6},-0.135\times 10^{5})\)

The value of this integral is \(−1615.5 K\), and by Eq. (4.19),

\( \Delta H^{\circ }=\Delta H^{\circ }_{0}+R\int_{T_{0}}^{T}{\frac{\Delta C^{\circ }_{P}}{R}dT }\) (4.19)

 

\( \Delta H^{\circ }=-90,135+8.314(-1615.5)=-103,566 J\)
