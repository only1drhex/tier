Guest Question.

Calculate the on state and off state power loss for the MOSFET having following parameters: \( V_{d s}=240 V, I_{d}=4 A, t_{r}=2 mA, t_{f}=120 ns , I_{d s s}=2 mA , R_{d s(o n)}=0.2 \Omega\) , duty cycle D = 50%, \( F_{\text {switching }}=50 kHz\)

Admin
Solution

(i) Total period \( T=1 / f=20 \mu sec\)

(ii) Period \( T_{\text {on }}=T_{\text {off }}\) (for 50% duty cycle) \( =10 \mu sec\)

(iii) On-state loss, \( P_{ on }=I_{D}^{2} \cdot R _{ ds ( on )} \cdot\left(T_{ on } / T\right) =\left(4^{2} \times 0.2 \times 10 \times 10^{-6}\right) /\left(20 \times 10^{-6}\right)=1.6 W \)

(iv) Off-state loss, \( P_{\text {off }}=V_{ DS (\max )} \cdot I_{ dss }\left(T_{\text {off }} / T\right) =\left(240 \times 2 \times 10^{-3} \times 10 \times 10^{-6}\right) /\left(20 \times 10^{-6}\right)=0.240 W\)
