0.1 2000 200 0.077 600 300 794 1.1 873
0.1 3000 300 0.115 300 300 626 1.25 795
0.3 3000 900 0.346 750 300 878 1.1 966
0.5 2400 1200 0.462 375 300 668 1.25 835

, with a = 3, the equivalent radial load \(F_{e} is\)

\(F_{e} = [0.077(873)^{3} + 0.115(795)^{3} + 0.346(966)^{3} + 0.462(835)^{3}]^{1∕3} = 884 lbf\)
