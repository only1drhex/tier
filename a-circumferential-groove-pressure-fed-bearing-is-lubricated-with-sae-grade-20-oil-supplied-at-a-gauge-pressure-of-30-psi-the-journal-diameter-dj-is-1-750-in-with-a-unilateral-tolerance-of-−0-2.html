Guest Question.

A circumferential-groove pressure-fed bearing is lubricated with SAE grade 20 oil supplied at a gauge pressure of 30 psi. The journal diameter \(d_{j}\) is 1.750 in, with a unilateral tolerance of −0.002 in. The central circumferential bushing has a diameter \(d_{b}\) of 1.753 in, with a unilateral tolerance of+0.004 in. The \(l^{\prime} / d\) ratio of the two “half-bearings” that constitute the complete pressure-fed bearing is 1/2. The journal angular speed is 3000 rev/min, or 50 rev/s, and the radial steady load is 900 lbf. The external sump is maintained at 120◦F as long as the necessary heat transfer does not exceed 800 Btu/h.

(a) Find the steady-state average film temperature.

(b) Compare h0, \(T_{\max }, \text { and } P_{s t}\) with the Trumpler criteria.

(c) Estimate the volumetric side flow \(Q_{s}\) , the heat loss rate \(H_{\text {loss }}\), and the parasitic friction torque.

Admin
Solution

(a)

 

\(r=\frac{d_{j}}{2}=\frac{1.750}{2}=0.875 \text { in }\)

 

\(c_{\min }=\frac{\left(d_{b}\right)_{\min }-\left(d_{j}\right)_{\max }}{2}=\frac{1.753-1.750}{2}=0.0015 \text { in }\)

 

\(\text { Since } l^{\prime} / d=1 / 2, l^{\prime}=d / 2=r=0.875\) in. Then the pressure due to the load is

 

\(P=\frac{W}{4 r l^{\prime}}=\frac{900}{4(0.875) 0.875}=294 \text { psi }\)

 

The Sommerfeld number S can be expressed as

 

\(S=\left(\frac{r}{c}\right)^{2} \frac{\mu N}{P}=\left(\frac{0.875}{0.0015}\right)^{2} \frac{\mu^{\prime}}{\left(10^{6}\right)} \frac{50}{294}=0.0579 \mu^{\prime}\) (1)

 

We will use a tabulation method to find the average film temperature. The first trial average film temperature T¯f will be 170◦F. Using the Seireg curve fit of Table 12–1, we obtain
