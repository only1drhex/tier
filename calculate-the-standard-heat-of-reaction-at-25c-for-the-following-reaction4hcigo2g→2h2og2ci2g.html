Guest Question.

Calculate the standard heat of reaction at \(25^{\circ}C\) for the following reaction:

\( 4HCI(g)+O_{2}(g)\rightarrow 2H_{2}O(g)+2CI_{2}(g)\)
Admin
Solution

Standard heats of formation at 298.15 K from Table C.4 are:

\( HCI(g):-92,307 J H_{2}O(g):-241,818 J \)

The following combination gives the desired result:

\(\begin{array}{r c} \begin{matrix} 4HCL(g)\rightarrow 2H_2(g)+2CL_2(g) & \Delta H^{\circ }{298}= (4)(92,307) \\ 2H_2(g)+O_2(g)\rightarrow 2H_2O(g) & \Delta H{298}^{\circ }=(2)(-241,818) \end{matrix} \\ \hline \begin{matrix} 4HCL(g)+O_2(g)\rightarrow 2H_2O(g)2CL_2(g) & \Delta H_{298}^{\circ }=-114,408 \ J\end{matrix}\end{array}\)
