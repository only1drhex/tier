Guest Question.

Draw influence lines for the reactions at A and C and for the bending moment at E in the beam system shown in Fig. P.20.8. Note that the beam AB is supported on the lower beam at D by a roller.
If two 10 kN loads, 5 mapart, cross the upper beamAB, determine the maximum values of the reactions at A and C and the bending moment at E.
Ans. \(R_{ A }(\max )=16.7 kN , R_{ C }(\max )=17.5 kN , M_{ E }(\max )=58.3 kNm\).

Admin
Solution

\(R _{ A }\) IL:
With unit load at A,

\(R_{ A }=1\)

With unit load at D,

\(R_{ A }=0\)

With unit load at B,

\(R_{ A }=-\frac{1}{3}\)

The IL is shown in Fig. S.20.8(a).

\(R_{ C }\) IL:
With unit load at A,

\(R_{ C }=0\)

With unit load at D,

\(R_{ C } \times 20-1 \times 15=0, \text { i.e. } R_{ C }=\frac{3}{4}\)

With unit load at B,

\(R_{ D } \times 15-1 \times 20=0, \text { i.e. } R_{ D }=1.33\)

Then,

\(R_{ C } \times 20-1.33 \times 15=0 \text {, i.e. } R_{ C }=1\)

The IL is shown in Fig. S.20.8(b).

\(M_{ E }\) IL:

\(M_{ E }=R_{ C } \times 10-R_{ D } \times 5\)

With unit load at A,

\(R_{ C }=R_{ D }=0\) so that \(M_{ E }=0\)

With unit load at D,

\(M_{ E }=\frac{3}{4} \times 10-1 \times 5=2.5\)

With unit load at B,

\(M_{ E }=1 \times 10-1.33 \times 5=3.33\)

The IL is shown in Fig. S.20.8(c).

From the influence lines

\(R_{ A (\max )}=10 \times 1+10\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)=16.7 kN\)

 

\(R_{ C (\max )}=10\left(\frac{3}{4}\right)+10 \times 1=17.5 kN\)

 

\(M_{ E (\max )}=10 \times 2.5+10 \times 3.33=58.3 kNm\).
