Solution

These data are added (Table PT5.2), and the results are used to compute the mean [Eq. (PT5.1)]

\(\overline{y}=\frac{158.4}{24}= 6.6 \)

 

\( \overline{y}=\frac{\sum{y_{i}} }{n} \) (PT5.1)

As in Table PT5.2, the sum of the squares of the residuals is 0.217000 which can be used to compute the standard deviation [Eq. (PT5.2)]:

\(S_{y}=\sqrt{\frac{0.217000}{24-1} } = 0.097133 \)

 

\(S_{y}=\sqrt{\frac{S_{t}}{n-1} } \) (PT5.2)

the variance [Eq. (PT5.4)]:

\( S_{y}^{2}= 0.009435\)

 

\( S_{y}^{2}=\frac{\sum{(y_{i}-\overline{y} )^{2}} }{n-1} \) (PT5.4)

and the coefficient of variation [Eq. (PT5.5)]:

\(C.V.=\frac{0.097133}{6.6} \)100% = 1.47%

 

\(C.V.=\frac{S_{y}}{\overline{y} } \)100% (PT5.5)
