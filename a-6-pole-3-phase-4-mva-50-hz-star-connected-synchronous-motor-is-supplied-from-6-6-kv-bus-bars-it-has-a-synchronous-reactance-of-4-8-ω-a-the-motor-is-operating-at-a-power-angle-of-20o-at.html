Guest Question.

A 6-pole, 3-phase, 4 MVA, 50 Hz, star connected synchronous motor is supplied from 6.6 kV bus-bars. It has a synchronous reactance of \( 4.8 \Omega \):

(a) The motor is operating at a power angle of 20º at rated current. Find the excitation emf if the power factor is lagging leading.

(b) In part (a) find the mechanical power developed and power factor in each case.

Admin
Solution
\( V_{t}=\frac{6.6}{\sqrt{3}}=3.81 kV\)

 

\( I_{a}(\text { rated })=\frac{4 \times 10^{3}}{\sqrt{3} \times 6.6}=350 A\)

 

\( I_{a} \text { (rated) } X_{s}=350 \times 4.8\)

= 1680 V or 1.68 kV

(a) In the motor

\( E_{f}\) lags \( V_{t}\) by \( \delta=20^{\circ}\)

 

\( \bar{E}_{f}=V_{t} \angle 0^{\circ}-j \bar{I}_{a} X_{s}\)

The phasor diagram is drawn in Fig. 8.64

As \( I_{a} X_{s}=1.68 kV\) constant, there are two possible solutions.

From the geometry of triangle ABD

\( V_{t}^{2}+E_{f}^{2}-2 V_{t} E_{f} \cos \delta=\left(I_{a} X_{s}\right)^{2}\)

Substituting values

\( (3.81)^{2}+E_{f}^{2}-2 \times 3.81 E_{f} \cos 20^{\circ}=(1.68)^{2}\)

Or \( E_{f}^{2}-7.16 E_{f}+11.69=0\)

Its solutions are \( E_{f 1}=2.52 kV , \quad E_{f 2}=4.64 kV\)

Or \( E_{f 1}=4.36 kV\) (line), \( E_{f 2}=8.04 kV\) (line)

(b) \( E_{f 1}=2.52 kV\) ; motor under-excited case, pf lagging

\( P_{m}=3 \times\left(\frac{3.81 \times 2.52}{4.8}\right) \sin 20^{\circ}=2.052 MW\)

 

\( \sqrt{3} \times 6.6 \times 350 \cos \theta_{1}=2.052 \times 10^{3}\)

 

\( p_{f_{1}}=\cos \theta_{1}=0.513\) lagging

\( E_{f 2}=4.64 kV\) ; ; motor over-excited pf leading

\( P_{m}=3 \times\left(\frac{3.81 \times 4.64}{4.8}\right) \sin 20^{\circ}=3.779 MW\)

 

\( \sqrt{3} \times 6.6 \times 350 \cos \theta_{2}=3.779 \times 10^{3}\)

 

\( p_{f_{2}}=\cos \theta_{2}=0.944\) leading; \( \theta_{2} \) = 19.2°
